Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 20 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  21 February 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-february-20-2024

Bulkers: Capesizes increase owing to recent Newcastlemax En blocs, Post-Panamaxes decrease owing to recent sales such as FPMC B 102

Post-Panamax BC FPMC B 102 (106,700 DWT, Apr 2011, STX Dalian) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.50 mil, VV Value USD 17.36 mil

Panamax BC Yasa Fortune (82,800 DWT, May 2006, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 12.7 mil, VV Value USD 12.70 mil

Supramax BC SSI Challenger (56,000 DWT, Sep 2004, Mitsui Tamano) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 10.7 mil, VV Value USD 10.40 mil

Tankers: Tanker values firmed slightly across the board, aside from the further increase of older tonnage MR values

MR2 (Chem/Product) Dong A Triton (50,500 DWT, Jan 2015, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 37.25 mil, VV Value USD 38.88 mil

MR2 Ardmore Seafarer (50,000 DWT, Jun 2010, Onomichi Dockyard) sold to Great Eastern Shipping for USD 27.10 mil (SS/DD Passed), VV Value USD 24.77 mil

MR2 (Chem/Product) Torm Platte (47,000 DWT, May 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Undisclosed buyers for USD 22 mil (SS/DD passed), VV Value USD 20.45 mil

Containers: Secondhand values appreciated across the board supported by a strengthened charter market

Panamax Jonathan Swift & Jack London (4,250 TEU, July 2010, Jiangsu New Yangzijiang) sold to MPCC in an en bloc deal for USD 17.6 mil (TCs attached until March 2025), VV Value 20.62 mil and 20.6 mil
