Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 29 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  29 May 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-29-2024

Bulkers: S&P Values remain mostly stable with small gains for Supramax vessels

Capesize BC El Grasso (180,200 DWT, Jul 2012, Imabari) sold to Agricore Shipping for USD 35.75 mil, VV Value USD 36.2 mil

Panamax BC Genesis (81,300 DWT, May 2012, Sungdong) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 22.5 mil, VV Value USD 23.1 mil

Supramax BC Marylaki (58,100 DWT, Jul 2010, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 16 mil

Tankers:Tanker values firming last week owing to improved earnings and a number of recent sales

LR1 UOG Aeolos (73,400 DWT, May 2009, New Times Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 28.8 mil, VV Value USD 27.77 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) Dee4 Larch (49,700 DWT, Aug 2016, Hyundai Vietnam Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Danish buyers for USD 41 mil, VV Value USD 40.99 mil

Containers:Secondhand values appreciated across the board driven by higher earnings

Handy M Wilmington (1,577 TEU, Feb 2010, Imbari) sold to Tanto Intim Line for USD 13.7 mil, VV Value USD 15.03 mil
