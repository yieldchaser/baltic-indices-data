Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 06 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  06 February 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-february-06-2024

Bulkers:Bulker Values stayed mostly stable across the board

Capesize BC Royal Iole (180,100 DWT, Apr 2009, Imabari) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 24.10 mil, VV value USD 23.59 mil

Panamax BC Navios Orbiter (76,600 DWT, Feb 2004, Imabari) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 9.20 mil, VV Value USD 9.75 mil

Supramax BC Rui Fi Sheng (53,100 DWT, May 2003, Oshima) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 8.60 mil, VV Value USD 8.58 mil

Tankers:Older VLCC tonnage decreased last week, whilst mid age Suezmax values increased due to the sale of Front Odin

Suezmax Front Odin (156,000 DWT, May 2010, Jiangsu Rongsheng) sold to NGM Energy for USD 45.50 mil, VV Value USD 44.91 mil

Aframax Koro Sea (105,900 DWT, Feb 2008, Namura) sold to Unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 39 mil, VV Value USD 38.45 mil

MR1 (Chem/Product) Maersk Kalea (38,900 DWT, Mar 2004, Gunagzhou CSSC Longxue Shipbuilding) sold to Unknown UAE buyers for USD 12.50 mil (SS/DD Due), VV Value USD 13.56 mil

Containers:Asset prices continued to appreciate across the board supported by higher earnings

No notable sales last week
