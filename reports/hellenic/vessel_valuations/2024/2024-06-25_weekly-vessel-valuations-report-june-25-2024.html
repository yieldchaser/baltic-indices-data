Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 25 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  25 June 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-25-2024

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report25/06/2024

Bulkers:S&P Values Bulker values increased for Supramax and Ultramax vessels owing to sales of vessels such as Beate Oldendorff and Velvet

Ultramax BC Beate Oldendorff (62,600 DWT, Mar 2020, Oshima) sold to unknown European buyers for USD 38.30 mil, VV Value USD 37.90 mil

Ultramax BC Velvet (62,600 DWT, Nov 2018, Oshima) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 36.00 mil, VV Value USD 35.70 mil

Supramax BC Bright Hero (55,600 DWT, Oct 2010, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.7 mil, VV Value USD 17.80 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mainly stable across the board again despite the observed fluctuations in earnings

LR1 Avra Patros (75,000 DWT, Aug 2008, Sungdong) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 29.75 mil, VV Value USD 29.52 mil

MR2 CSC Progress (45,800 DWT, Oct 2007, Jinling Shipyard Jiangsu) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 21 mil, VV Value USD 21.42 mil

J19 Stainless Steel Songa Challenge (20,000 DWT, Aug 2009, Usuki Zosensho) sold to Heung-A Shipping for USD 23 mil, VV Value USD 22.20 mil

Containers:Secondhand values appreciated across the board, with impressive gains for 15YO Panamax in response to sales

Panamax Northern Dependant and Northern Democrat (3,534 TEU, Feb 2008-Sep 2009, Shanghai Shipyard) sold to MPC Containers in an en bloc deal for USD 47.25 mil (Against pre-agreed 36-month TCs), VV Value USD 43.1 mil
