LR2 Mare Oriens (110,500 DWT, Sep 2008, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 42 mil (SS/DD Passed), VV Value USD 42.36 mil

MR1 Paprika (40,100 DWT, Jan 2003, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Undisclosed buyers for USD 15 mil, VV Value USD 14.08

Containers:Another week of positive gains across the board for Container values supported by higher earnings
