Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 04 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  04 June 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-04-2024

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report05/06/2024

Bulkers:S&P Values increased for Panamax vessels, most notably for older tonnage owing to sale of vessels such as Bravery

Capesize BC Eastern Windflower (175,400 DWT, Jun 2010, New Times Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 28.00 mil, VV Value USD 27.1 mil

Panamax BC Bravery (76,600 DWT, Sep 2004, Imabari) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.5 mil, VV Value USD 11.8 mil

Handy BC (Open Hatch) Dino (33,400 DWT, Oct 2009, Shin Kochi) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 13.4 mil, VV Value USD 13.5 mil

Tankers:Tanker values mainly stable across the board with older tonnage MRs firming once again

No notable sales last week

Containers:Secondhand values firmed this week for most types and ages, the exception being mid-age Handy and Sub Panamax vessels declining c.6%

Handy M Wilmington (1,577 TEU, Feb 2010, Imabari) sold to Tanto Intim Line for USD 13.5 mil, VV Value USD 14.4 mil
