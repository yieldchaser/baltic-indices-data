inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report14/05/2024

Bulkers:S&P Values for bulkers remain stable this week

Capesize BC Corinthian Phoenix (179,200 DWT, Sep 2009, Hyundai Ulsan) sold to Hayfin Capital for USD 30 mil, VV Value USD 29.40 mil

Panamax BC Cuma (82,500 DWT, Jan 2010, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 20 mil (DD/SS Passed), VV Value USD 18.90 mil

Supramax BC Sibulk Tradition (53,200 DWT, May 2008, Iwagi Zosen) sold to unknown middle eastern buyers for USD 14.5 mil, VV Value USD 14.70 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mainly stable with older tonnage MRs softening slightly

MR2 (Chem/Prod) Stavanger Pioneer (50,000 DWT, Nov 2019, Hyundai Vietnam Shipbuilding) sold to Coral Shipping for USD 47.8 mil, VV Value USD 48.13 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) Grace Fortuna (47,800 DWT, Aug 2007, STX Offshore) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 23 mil, VV Value USD 22.2 mil

Containers:Container values firmed across the board from improved earnings

Feedermax Ara Amsterdam (1,025 TEU, Jun 2010, Fujian Mawei Shipbuilding) sold to Danz and Tietjens Schiffahrtsgruppe (BWTS) undisclosed, VV Value USD 9.18 mil
