Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, March 12 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  12 March 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-march-12-2024

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report12/03/2024

Bulkers: Mid-age Bulk carrier values firmed significantly after strong sale prices of vessels such as Epic and Panayiota K

Capesize Epic (180,100 DWT, Dec 2010, Daehan) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 32 mil (DD Passed), VV Value USD 31.12 mil

Post-Panamax BC Panayiota K (92,000 DWT, Apr 2010, Sungdong) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 20.45 mil, VV Value USD 19.04 mil

Supramax BC Andromeda (61,500 DWT, Jan 2011, Oshima) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 21.0 mil (DD Passed), VV Value USD 19.80 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mostly stable last week, whilst older MR tonnage softened

Aframax Sea Hazel (106,100 DWT, Jan 2004, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to Unknown Middle Eastern buyers for USD 29 mil (SS/DD Passed), VV Value USD 30.84 mil

MR2 5x Scorpio (50,000 DWT, 2015, SPP) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 195 mil, VV Value USD 199.07 mil

Containers:Secondhand values appreciated across the board driven by strengthened earnings, excluding mid-age Panamax in response to a recent sale

Handy Starship Leo (1,891 TEU, Feb 2013, Hyundai Mipo) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 16 mil (BWTS), VV Value USD 15.96 mil
