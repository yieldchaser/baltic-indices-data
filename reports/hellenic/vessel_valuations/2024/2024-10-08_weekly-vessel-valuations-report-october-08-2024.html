Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, October 08 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  08 October 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-08-2024

Bulkers:S&P values have decreased notably for secondhand Panamax and Kamsarmax vessels

Capesize BC Oriental Navigator (172,900 DWT, Feb 1999, NKK) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.9 mil, VV Value USD 12.19mil

Panamax BC Pan Viva (75,000 DWT, Oct 2010, Penglai Zhongbai Jinglu) sold to Undiclosed buyer for USD 15.00 mil, VV Value USD 14.37 mil

Supramax BC Louisiana Mama (58,100 DWT, Aug 2012, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to Unknown Far Eastern buyers for USD 19 mil, VV Value USD 18.52 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remaining stable last week with uptick for older tonnage Aframax and MR values

Aframax Lambada (104,900 DWT, Jul 2006, Samsung) sold to undisclosed buyers (DD Due) for USD 30.3 mil, VV Value USD 31.48 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) Hafnia Andromeda (50,300 DWT, May 2011, Guangzhou CSSC Longxue Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers (DD Passed) for USD 31 mil, VV Value USD 29.02 mil

Containers:Container values remain stable this week, with some small gains for midage Panamaxes, and older Feedermaxes

4 Post Panamaxes Livorno Express, Detroit Express, Genoa Express, Barcelona Express (3,832 TEU, 2014, Hanjin Subic) sold to MPC for USD 180 mil en bloc inc TC, VV Value USD 183.11 mil combined
