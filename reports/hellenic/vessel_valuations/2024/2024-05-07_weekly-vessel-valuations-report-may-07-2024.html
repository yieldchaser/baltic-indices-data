Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 07 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  07 May 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-07-2024

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report07/05/2024

Bulkers:S&P Values for mid-age Ultramax and Supramax increased last week owing to sales such as Florentine Oetker

Capesize BC Urja (180,700 DWT, Aug 2013, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to Hayfin Capital for USD 38.00 mil VV Value USD 38.30 mil

Panamax BC Cuma (82,500 DWT, Apr 2006, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 15.0 mil VV Value USD 13.90 mil

Ultramax BC Florentine Oetker (63,000 DWT, May 2017, Imabari) sold to Ince Denizcilik for USD 32.85 mil VV Value USD 31.40 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mainly stable with older tonnage LR1s firming

Suezmax Euronike (164,600 DWT, Sep 2005, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 40.5 mil, VV Value USD 40.62 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) STI Manhattan (50,000 DWT, Mar 2015, SPP) sold to KSS Line for USD 41 mil, VV Value USD 40.36 mil

MR2 Dai Minh (47,100 DWT, Jan 2004, Onomichi Dockyard) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 15 mil, VV Value USD 15.14 mil

Containers:Container values firmed across the board from improved earnings, except for vintage Feedermax that lost up to 1.2%

No notable sales
