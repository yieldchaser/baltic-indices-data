Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 18 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 June 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-18-2024

Bulkers:S&P Values for Handysize BCs increase across the board owing to sales such as Sider Eva Maria

Ultramax BC Swansea (63,500 DWT, Jul 2015, Yangzhou Dayang) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 25.00 mil, VV Value USD 24.42 mil

Supramax BC Panagia Kanala (56,600 DWT, Jun 2012, COSCO Zhoushan) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.00 mil, VV Value USD 16.6 mil

Handy BC (Open Hatch) Sider Eva Maria (39,000 DWT, Mar 2014, Chengxi) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 21.0 mil, VV Value USD 19.27 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mainly stable across the board again

LR2 Alpine Confidence (107,600 DWT, Mar 2010, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 43.80 mil, VV Value USD 45.57 mil

LR2 Norvic Monia (105,300 DWT, Sep 2008, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 42.3 mil, VV Value USD 41.93 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) Gunmetal Jack (50,100 DWT, Aug 2009, SPP) sold to Besiktas Likid Tasimacilik for USD 28 mil, VV Value USD 27.97 mil

Containers:Secondhand values appreciated across all ages and sizes with few exceptions

No notable sales last week
