Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 27 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  27 February 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-february-27-2024

Bulkers: Modern Supramaxes see values firming, with small gains on Capesizes too

Capesize Sapientza (177,700 DWT, Nov 2008, Jiangnan Shanghai HI) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 22.00 mil (SS/DD passed), VV Value USD 21.65 mil

Ultramax BC Santa Johanna (61,300 DWT, Mar 2017, Shin Kurushima) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 30.1 mil, VV Value USD 29.63 mil

Handy BC Lucky Glory (32,300 DWT, Dec 2007, Kanda) sold to unknown Turkish for USD 10.55 mil, VV Value USD 11.00 mil

Tankers: Tanker values firmed across the board, with the biggest increase seen in older tonnage Suezmaxes due to the sale of Raptor

VLCC Eco Seas (300,000 DWT, Oct 2016, Daewoo) sold to Undisclosed buyers for USD 98.50 mil, VV Value USD 97.12 mil

Suezmax Raptor (159,400 DWT, Oct 2007, Universal) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 46.50 mil, VV Value USD 45.39 mil

MR1 (Chem/Prod) Leon Hermes (40,200 DWT, Dec 2008, SLS) sold to Spring Marine Management for USD 23 mil (SS/DD Passed), VV Value USD 21.85 mil

25K Stainless Steel Beatrice (25,900 DWT, Nov 2013, Asakawa) sold to DM Shipping for USD 29 mil (DD Passed), VV Value USD 30.60 mil

Containers: Secondhand values appreciated from Handy’s up to ULCVs supported by higher earnings. Mid to vintage Feedermax was the exception depreciating in response to a 16YO sale

Feedermax Mistral (862 TEU, Feb 2008, Sietas) sold to UAE buyer for USD 9 mil, VV Value 9.93 mil
