Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, April 02 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  02 April 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-april-02-2024

Bulkers: Larger Bulk Carriers values stabilised whilst smaller Handysize increased owing to sales of vessels such as Sider Harmony

Capesize BCs Atlantic Lion, Atlantic Dragon, Atlantic Tiger (210,000 DWT, 2020, SWS) sold in en bloc deal to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 213 mil, VV Value USD 213.60 mil

Supramax BC Desert Unity (54,000 DWT, Oct 2006, Taizhou Kouan) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 10.10 mil, VV Value USD 9.90 mil

Handy BC Sider Harmony (38,600 DWT, Feb 2019, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold SS/DD Passed to unknown Middle Eastern buyers for USD 28.50 mil, VV Value USD 26.70 mil

Tankers:Tanker values mostly stable, apart from a decrease in older tonnage VLCC

MR2 (Chem/Prod) UOG Sparta (50,000 DWT, Mar 2009, SPP) sold to unknown buyers for USD 24.35 mil, VV Value USD 25.17 mil

Small Clean Tanker (Chem/Prod) Vestholmen (17,500 DWT, Mar 2009, Samho Tongyoung) sold to unknown buyers for USD 15 mil, VV Value USD 13.81 mil

Containers: Post Panamax values firmed strongly last week due to the recent sale of Navios Spring whilst older Feedermax values softened

Panamax Navios Spring (3,450 TEU, Jan 2007, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Greek marine Transports Shipping for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 16.00 mil
