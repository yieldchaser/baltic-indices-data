Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, March 26 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  26 March 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-march-26-2024

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report26/03/2024

Bulkers: Mid age Panamax bulk carrier values continue to firm, owing to sale of Key Guardian

Panamax BC Key Guardian (83,500 DWT, Apr 2011, Sanoyas) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 23.70 mil VV Value USD 22.07 mil

Supramax BC NZ Hangzhou (56,700 DWT, Jun 2012, Qingshan Shipyard) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 14.70 mil, VV Value USD 14.88 mil

Handy BC Asian Bulker (36,200 DWT, Apr 2017, Shikoku Dockyard) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 24.00 mil, VV Value USD 23.27 mil

Tankers: Tanker values mostly stable, with the exception of a decrease in older VLCC tonnage due to sale of C Vision

VLCC Nectar, Noble & Newton (307,300 DWT, Jan 2008-Feb 2009, Dalian Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Chinese buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 154.9 mil, VV Value USD 154.21 mil

VLCC C Vision (314,000 DWT, Jun 2004, Samsung) sold to unknown buyers for USD 33.5 mil, VV Value USD 38.75 mil

MR2 (Chem/Prod) STI Le Rocher & STI Larvotto (52,000 DWT, Jun 2013-Jul 2013, Hyundai Mipo) sold to unknown buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 72.3 mil, VV Value USD 72.61 mil

Containers: Secondhand values generally appreciated across the board driven by firmed earnings

Post Panamax Lyon II and Paris II (6,447 TEU, Jun/Apr 2001, Hanjin Heavy Ind) sold en bloc to MSC for USD 40 mil, VV Value 33.9 mil
