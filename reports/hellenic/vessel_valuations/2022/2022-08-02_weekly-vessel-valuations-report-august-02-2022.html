Bulkers:Bulker values have softened

Panamax Bonanza YR (76,500 DWT, Aug 2006, Tsuneishi Zosen) sold to Middle Eastern buyers for USD 16.50 mil, VV Value USD 18.36 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Supramax Sophia K (55,600 DWT, Aug 2011, Mitsui Tamano) sold to Middle Eastern buyers for USD 22.90 mil, VV Value USD 21.94 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Handy BC Maria L (28,400 DWT, Feb 1998, Hakodate Dock) sold to unknown Syrian buyers for USD 7.30 mil, VV Value USD 8.12 mil – SS Due.

Handy BC Global Aquarius (28,300 DWT, Jan 2010, Imabari) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 14.50 mil, VV Value USD 14.77 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Tankers:Suezmax and LR1 values have firmed

Aframax Oracle (105,400 DWT, Jul 2008, Sumitomo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 28.75 mil, VV Value USD 28.92 mil.

Handy Chemical Tanker (Methanol) Patagonian Mystic (49,400 DWT, Jul 2005, Naikai Setoda) sold to Far Eastern buyers for USD 13.80 mil, VV Value USD 13.78 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Containers:Container values have remained stable

Post Panamax Cont Cap Capricorn (3,884 TEU, Oct 2013, Zhejiang Shipbuilding) sold to CMA CGM for USD 75.00 mil, VV Value USD 77.23 mil.
