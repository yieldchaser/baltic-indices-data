inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report28/06/2022

Bulkers:Bulker values have softened

Supramax Bao Wealth (53,600 DWT, Jun 2005, Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.80 mil, VV Value USD 13.82 mil.

Handy BC Eva Bulker (38,200 DWT, May 2012, Naikai) sold to Devbulk Shipping for USD 23.00 mil, VV Value USD 23.34 mil – SS Due.

Handy BC Super Valentina (33,400 DWT, Feb 2013, Shin Kurushima Onishi) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 22.00 mil, VV Value USD 21.03 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Tankers:Mid-age Aframax and LR1 values have firmed

VLCCs Hunter Freya and Hunter Idun (300,000 DWT, Mar/Jul 2020, Daewoo) sold to DAO Shipping for USD 191.00 mil, VV Value USD 188.59 mil – En Bloc.

VLCC Hunter Disen and Hunter Frigg (300,000 DWT, Jun/Aug 2020, Daewoo) sold to Maran Tankers for USD 192.00 mil, VV Value USD 190.33 mil – En Bloc.

Aframax Maran Sagitta (105,100 DWT, Sep 2009, Hyundai Heavy Ind) sold to Performance Shipping Inc for USD 27.58 mil, VV Value USD 25.12 mil – DD Due, BWTS Fitted.

LR1 Energy Centurion (75,000 DWT, Aug 2008, Sungdong) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 18.00 mil, VV Value USD 16.80 mil.

MR2 Energy Panther (46,600 DWT, Nov 2008, Sungdong) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.00 mil, VV Value USD 16.66 mil.

MR1 (Chem / Product) Baltic Favour (37,100 DWT, Feb 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.10 mil, VV Value USD 11.18 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Small Clean Tanker (Chem/Prod) Maersk Borneo (29,000 DWT, Apr 2007, CSSC OME) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.80 mil, VV Value USD 10.87 mil – SS Passed.

Containers:Older Handy Container values have firmed

Panamax Cont Eastaway Malmsey (3,421 TEU, Nov 2011, Rongcheng Shenfei Shipbuilding) sold to Safeen Feeders for USD 65.00 mil, VV Value USD 65.72 mil.
