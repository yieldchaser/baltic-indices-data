Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, January 18 2022

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 January 2022  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-january-18-2022

Tanker:Tanker values have softened.

VLCC Tsushima (310,400 DWT, Dec 2008, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to Greek buyers for USD 36.40 mil, VV value USD 37.33 mil – SS/DD Passed.

Bulker:Supramax and Older Panamax values have softened.

Supramax Diamond Stars (55,400 DWT, Apr 2011, Bulyard) sold for USD 17.00 mil, VV value USD 17.03 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Handy Bulker Crimson Princess (38,400 DWT, Jun 2012, Naikai Setoda) sold to Greek buyers 19.00 mil, VV value USD 21.81 mil – SS/DD Due.

Handy Bulker Xing Yuan Hai (34,400 DWT, Dec 2015, Namura) sold to Tufton Oceanic Assets 23.75 mil, VV value USD 23.88 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Container:Handy values have firmed.

Sub Panamax St Ever (2,533 TEU, Aug 2011, Naikai Setoda) sold to Wan Hai Lines for USD 46.50 mil, VV value USD 39.41 mil.

Sub Panamax Cardiff Trader (2,526, Mar 2003, Kvaerner Warnow Werft) sold to MSC for USD 30.00 mil, VV value USD 29.80 mil.

Handy Container Mount Gough (1,730 TEU, Oct 2016, Zhejiang Ouhua) sold to CMA CGM for USD 42.20 mil, VV value USD 35.02 mil – SS/DD Due.
