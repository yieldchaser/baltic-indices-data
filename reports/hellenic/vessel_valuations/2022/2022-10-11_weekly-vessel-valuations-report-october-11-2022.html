Supramax Cas Avanca (55,600 DWT, Oct 2009, Hyundai Vietnam Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.30 mil, VV Value USD 16.21 mil – BWTS fitted.

Suezmax Nordic Cosmos (160,000 DWT, Jan 2003, Samsung) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 21.00 mil, VV Value USD 22.56 mil – SS Due

MR2 (Chem / Product) Baltic Advance (37,300 DWT, Feb 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.50 mil, VV Value USD 15.46 mil.

MR1 (Chem / Product) Synergy (34,700 DWT, Jan 2020, Fujian Mawei Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 31.00 mil, VV Value USD 28.11 mil – DD Due.

Containers:Container values have softened due to earnings.

Panamax Cont Genova (4,253 TEU, Apr 2007, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold to MSC for USD 45.00 mil, VV Value USD 48.78 mil – SS Passed.
