Bulkers:Bulker values have softened

Panamax Darya Lok (81,900 DWT, Jun 2012, Daewoo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 21.50 mil, VV Value USD 22.12 mil – SS Passed.

Panamax LT Ocean Star (75,400 DWT, Jul 2005, Universal) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.00 mil, VV Value USD 13.62 mil – DD Passed.

Handy BC (Open Hatch) Interlink Activity (38,700 DWT, Sep 2015, Taizhou Kouan Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 21.00 mil, VV Value USD 19.45 mil – BWTS fitted.

Handy BC Super Caroline (33,400 DWT, Aug 2007, Shin Kochi) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 13.70 mil, VV Value USD 12.80 mil – BWTS fitted.

Tankers:Tanker values have firmed

MR1 (Chem / Product) Vallermosa (40,200 DWT, Jan 2003, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.00 mil, VV Value USD 13.32 mil – SS Due.

Small Clean Tankers (Chem/Prod) GS Forward and GS Future (17,500 DWT, Dec 2008, Feb 2009, Samho Tongyoung) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 24.00 mil, VV Value USD 21.09 mil – En Bloc.
