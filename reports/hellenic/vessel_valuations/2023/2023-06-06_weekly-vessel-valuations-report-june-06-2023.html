inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report06/06/2023

Bulkers: Bulker Values have remained stable

Ultramax BC Great Venture (61,000 DWT, Sept 2019, Dalian COSCO) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 30.40 mil, VV Value USD 30.62 mil

Handy BC Straits Breeze (31,600 DWT, Apr 2009, Saiki) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 13.00 mil, VV Value USD 13.02 mil

Tankers: Modern VLCC values have softened and will continue to do so, due to the new benchmark sale of Maria P Lemos

VLCC Maria P Lemos (319,200 DWT, Oct 2018, Hyundai HI) sold to DHT Holdings for USD 94.50 mil, VV Value USD 103.07 mil – SS/DD Due

Suezmax Front Njord (156,000 DWT, Aug 2010, Jiangsu Ronsheng) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 44.50 mil, VV Value USD 41.11 mil – DD Due

Post Panamax (Clean) Amazon Beauty (72,900 DWT, Jan 2004, Hyundai HI) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 19.50 mil, VV Value 18.66 mil

Containers: Container values have continued to firm marginally as a result of firming rates

No notable sales this week.
