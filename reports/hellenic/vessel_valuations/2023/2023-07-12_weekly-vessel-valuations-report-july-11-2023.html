Tankers: VLCC values expected to soften in the next week

MR2 Yongsheng Ocean (45,000 DWT, Jun 1997, Halla) sold to Unidisclosed buyers for USD 7.20 mil (BWTS), VV Value USD 8.40 mil

Containers: Post Panamax Containers have firmed over 9% as a result of the en bloc resales bought by MSC

Post Panamax London Bridge and Dubai Bridge (8,000 TEU, Mar 2024, Hyundai HI) sold to MSC in an enbloc deal for USD 200 mil, VV Value USD 191.47 mil
