Hellenic Shipping News  |  14 March 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-march-14-2023

MR2 Atlantica Bay (46,800 DWT, Nov 2007, Sungdong) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 22.00 mil, VV Value USD 20.80 mil – SS/DD Passed.

MR1 (Chem / Product) Inviken and Utviken (37,900 DWT, Apr 2009, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 40.00 mil, VV Value USD 41.35 mil.
