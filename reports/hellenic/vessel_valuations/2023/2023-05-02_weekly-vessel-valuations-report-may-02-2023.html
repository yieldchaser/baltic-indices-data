Bulkers: Capesize values have softened, whilst Supramax values have firmed

Capesize CHS Splendor (170,000 DWT, Jan 2006, IHI) sold to Lila Global for USD 16.30 mil, VV Value USD 17.84 mil – BWTS

Tankers: Tanker values have remained stable, aside from the softening of older tonnage Suezmaxes

Suezmax Ridgebury John Zipser (164,800 DWT, May 2009, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 45.00 mil, VV Value USD 46.13 mil – Scrubber.

MR2 Bahri Jasmine (49,000 DWT, Apr 2005, Daewoo) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 18.30 mil, VV Value USD 18.81 mil – DD Due.

Small Chemical Bunga Lilac (19,900 DWT, Apr 2011, Fukuoka) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 21.90 mil, VV Value USD 23.81 mil – BWTS.

Containers: Container values have firmed, aside from the softening of Panamax values
