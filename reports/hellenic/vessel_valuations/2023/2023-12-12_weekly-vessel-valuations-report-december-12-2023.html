Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, December 12 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  12 December 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-december-12-2023

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report12/12/2023

Bulkers: An active S&P market for Capesizes sees values for new vessels continue to climb owing to high sale prices achieved for recent sales such as Herun China

Capesize BC Herun China (181,100 DWT, Jan 2017, SWS) sold to Union Maritime for USD 41 mil, VV Value USD 40.43mil

Capesize BCs Mineral Brugge, Mineral Temse, Mineral Destelbergen (175,000 DWT, Jun 2010/Feb 2011, New Times) sold in en bloc deal to undisclosed buyers for USD 61.85 mil VV Value USD 65.11

Panamax BC Navios Hyperion (75,700 DWT, Feb 2004, Sanoyas) sold to unknown Far Eastern buyers for USD 9 mil, VV Value USD 9.75 mil

Handy BC Atlantic Altamira (43,400 DWT, Jun 2017, Qingshan) sold to unknown South Korean buyers for USD 23 mil, VV Value USD 22.25 mil

Tankers: Resale VLCCs increased last week as a result of Niawa & Diyala being sold to Bahri for 114 mil each

VLCC Ninawa & Diyala (320,500 DWT, Mar/Apr 2019, Samsung) sold to Bahri for USD 228 mil, VV Value USD 216.83 mil

VLCC Athenian Freedom (319,100 DWT, Mar 2013, Hyundai HI) sold to Thenamaris for USD 73.50 mil, VV Value USD 75.22 mil

LR1 Leon Apollon (75,500 DWT, Jul 2009, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 28.50 mil, VV Value USD 28.68 mil

MR2 (Chem/Product) Nord Sustainable & Nord Supreme (49,600 DWT, Feb-May 2015, STX Offshore) sold to Unknown Greek buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 78 mil, VV Value USD 76.86 mil

MR2 (Chem/Product) Tradewind Energy (47,100 DWT, Feb 2009, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 24.10 mil (SS/DD Due), VV Value USD 24.35 mil

Containers: ULCV, New Panamax, and Post Panamax resales firmed 0.5%, 0.5%, and 4.8%

Panamax Zhong Gu Fu Jian (4,860 TEU, Feb 2010, Daewoo-Mangalia) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 18 mil, VV Value USD 20.31 mil
