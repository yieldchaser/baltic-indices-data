Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, October 24 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  24 October 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-24-2023

Bulkers

Capesize values have firmed across the board whereas other bulkers remain stable

Capesize BC Cymona Iron (176,500 DWT, Jan 2011, SWS) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 26.0 mil, VV Value USD 25.02 mil

Panamax BC Royal Fukuyama (82,200 DWT, Jul 2013, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to Kyla Shipping for USD 22.80 mil, VV Value USD 22.50 mil

Ultramax BC Santa Francesca (61,300 DWT, Jan 2016, Shin Kasado) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 26.80 mil, VV Value USD 26.1 mil

Handy BC St Gregory (32,700 DWT, Jun 2010, Jiangmen Nanyang) sold to Madmar Shipping for USD 10.50 mil, VV Value USD 10.73 mil

Tankers

Tanker values have remained stable last week

MR2 (Chem/Product) GH Austen (50,300 DWT, Mar 2009, CSSC OME) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 23 mil (BWTS), VV Value USD 22.39 mil

MR2 (Chem/Product) Nancy P, Jane S, Essie C, Marie S (50,000 DWT, 2015-16, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Torm in en bloc deal for USD 150 mil, VV Value USD 154.18 mil

Containers

Last week saw continued gradual decline in second-hand values, with mid-age/older Post Panamax vessels experiencing a 2-3% decline

Handy/Feedermax AS Roberta, As Rafaela (1,440 TEU, 2006/07, P+S Werften) & As Flora (1,223 TEU, Mar 2005, Peene Werft) sold to Arkas Denizcilik in enbloc deal for USD 21.7 mil, VV Value USD 20.78 mil

Handy Container A Kobe (1,800 TEU, Mar 2023, Jiangsu Yangzijiang) sold (resale) to unknown Greek buyers for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 25.50 mil
