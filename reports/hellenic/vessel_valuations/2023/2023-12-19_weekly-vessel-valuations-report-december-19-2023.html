Bulkers: Values remained stable for the bulker sector

Capesize BC Iron Miracle (180,600 DWT, Jan 2011, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to Costamare for USD 27 mil, VV Value USD 27.06 mil

Panamax BC Glory Amsterdam (77,700 DWT, Apr 2006, Oshima) sold unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.50 mil, VV Value USD 11.92 mil

Ultramax BC Unity Endeavour (61,600 DWT, Aug 2014, Nantong COSCO) sold inc. TC to undisclosed buyers for USD 23 mil, VV Value USD 22.41 mil

Handy BC Adventure (33,800 DWT, Jan 2011, Samjin) sold to Costamare for USD 11.4 mil, VV Value USD 10.77 mil

Tankers: Modern VLCCs increased further due to the sale of Delos

VLCC Delos (318,600 DWT, Jun 2019. Daewoo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 116 mil (SS/DD Due), VV Value USD 113.78 mil

MR2 Jag Prabha (48,000 DWT, Oct 2004, Iwagi Zosen) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14 mil, VV Value USD 15.38 mil

Containers: Modern and mid-age values depreciated across all size segments impacting Sub Panamax and Handy’s the most losing c.3.6% on average. 10YO Feedermax bucked the trend firming 4.7% w-o-w

No notable sales last week
