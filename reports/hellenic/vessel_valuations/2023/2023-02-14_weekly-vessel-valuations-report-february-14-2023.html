Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 14 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  14 February 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-february-14-2023

Bulkers: Panamax values have firmed

Capesize Thalassini Astrid (179,800 DWT, Mar 2014, Tianjin Xingang) sold to Tomini Shipping for USD 34.00 mil, VV Value USD 28.49 mil – Scrubber.

Panamax Navios Libertas (75,500 DWT, Aug 2007, STX Offshore) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 13.80 mil, VV Value USD 14.08 mil – SS/DD Passed.

Ultramax Springfield (63,700 DWT, Mar 2020, COSCO Shipping Heavy Industry Yangzhou) sold to Unknown USA for USD 30.75 mil, VV Value USD 26.20 mil – DD Due.

Supramax Serenitas N (56,600 DWT, Mar 2011, Yangzhou Guoyu Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 12.50 mil, VV Value USD 11.88 mil – BWTS.

Handy BC (Open Hatch) Shanghai Pearl (35,900 DWT, Jan 2011, Hyundai Mipo) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 14.00 mil, VV Value USD 14.99 mil – BWTS.

Handy BC Belle Etoile (28,200 DWT, Oct 2014, I-S) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 13.00 mil, VV Value USD 13.23 mil – BWTS.

Tankers: Tanker values have remained stable

VLCC Adventure (308,800 DWT, Oct 2005, Samsung) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 52.50 mil, VV Value USD 52.87 mil – Scrubber.

Aframax Atlantis (105,400 DWT, Sep 2008, Sumitomo) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 38.00 mil, VV Value USD 38.74 mil – BWTS.

LR1 Hafnia Columbia and Hafnia Kronborg (76,600 DWT, Jan 2007, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold to undisclosed buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 44 mil, VV Value USD 43.69 mil – BWTS.

MR2 Super Emerald (50,300 DWT, Sep 2006, SLS) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 17.50 mil, VV Value USD 17.20 mil – BWTS.

MR2 Pink Coral (49,500 DWT, Apr 2003, STX Offshore) sold to Unknown UAE for USD 13.00 mil, VV Value USD 15.26 mil – SS/DD Due.

Containers: Container values have softened

Sub Panamax Northern Volition and Northern Vivacity (2,750 TEU, Nov 2005, Wadan Yards MTW) sold to undisclosed buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 26 mil, VV Value USD 23.96 mil

Feedermax TS Moji, TS Shanghai and TS Yokohama (962 – 1,096 TEU, Dec 2006 – Nov 2019, Dae Sun & Kyokuyo) sold to unknown Europeans in an en bloc deal for USD 40 mil, VV Value USD 41.34 mil

Feedermax Sky Pride (962 TEU, Jul 2005, Dae Sun) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.50 mil, VV Value USD 6.45 mil – DD Due.
