Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, April 28 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  29 April 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-april-28-2026

Bulkers:Bulker secondhand activity remained busy this week, with transactions spanning Panamax to Newcastlemaxtonnage and Chinese buyers featuring prominently across multiple segments. Resale appetite also remained evident, with two 64,100 DWT Ultramax newbuilds from New Dayang Shipbuilding sold to Niovis Shipping, though soft pricing resulted in a dip to our Ultramax resale values.

Capesizes RTM Carrier (205,500 DWT, 2012, Hanjin Subic) and RTM Zheng He (205,400 DWT, 2012, Hanjin Subic) sold to Chinese buyers, latter DD Passed, for USD 90 mil, VV Value USD 89 mil

Supramaxes Hull 1315 (64,100 DWT, 2027, New Dayang) and Hull 1316 (64,100 DWT, 2027, New Dayang) resold to Niovis Shipping for USD 73 mil, VV Value USD 73.6 mil

Supramax ASL Ixora (61,500 DWT, 2012, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 19.6 mil, VV Value USD 19.5 mil

Handysize KS Grace (36,300 DWT, 2014, Shikoku Dockyard) sold to Greek buyers for USD 16.95 mil, VV Value USD 17.1 mil

Tankers:The secondhand tanker market is showing broad strength this week, with values firming across mid-size and older tonnage in particular.

VLCC Kasagisan (308,000 DWT, Aug 2006, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 60 mil, VV Value USD 57.59 mil

LR2 STI Madison, STI Park & STI Sloane (110,000 DWT, 2014, Hyundai Samho HI) sold by Scorpio Tankers for USD 195 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 207 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) STI Aqua, STI Regina & STI Opera (50,000 DWT, 2014, SPP & Hyundai Mipo) sold to Besiktas Likid Tasimacilik for USD 105 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 105.08 mil

Containers:Second-hand values have remained flat this week as no new sales have come to light

No notable sales.
