Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, March 31 2026

in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 01/04/2026

Bulkers: Bulker secondhand activity remained active on a week-on-week basis, though the broader March picture tells a more cautious story. Sales are down 68% against the same month last year, as ongoing Middle East uncertainty has prompted many owners to adopt a wait-and-see approach. Values remained relatively stable across the Bulker sector. Exceptions to this included modern Panamax BCs, where values softened somewhat, and midage Supramaxes, where values firmed in response to strong pricing.

Panamax Gia Inspiration (85,000 DWT, 2022, CSSC Tianjin) sold by CSSC Shipping for USD 33.3 mil, VV Value USD 33.3 mil

Panamax Agri Kinsale (77,200 DWT, 2009, Oshima) sold by CITIC for USD 15.1 mil, VV Value USD 15.0 mil

Supramax Xo Copenhagen (58,100 DWT, 2010, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 16.3 mil, VV Value USD 15.3 mil

Supramax Hony Future (56,700 DWT, 2012, Xiamen Shipbuilding) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 14.2 mil, VV Value USD 14.1 mil

Tankers: Tanker values held their firming trend across all tanker segments last week, with the Suezmax sector drawing notable attention on the back of strong S&P activity and continued earnings momentum.

Suezmax Seacross (163,300 DWT, Jan 2006, Hyundai Samho HI) sold by Thenamaris for USD 43.5 mil, VV Value USD 41.86 mil

Suezmax Nordic Skier (159,100 DWT, May 2005, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 40 mil, VV Value USD 39.33 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) High Seas (51,700 DWT, Mar 2012, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Spring Marine Management for USD 27.6 mil, VV Value USD 26.7 mil

Containers: The smaller segments have dominated sales and orders this week. With rates remaining stable, there is little impetus to sell, and older handy container values continue to benefit. Newbuilding values for the same have eased slightly following recent orders.

Handy Container Songa Wolf (1,732 TEU, Dec 2007, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to MSC for USD 19.5 mil, VV Value USD 19 mil.

Sub Panamax Container Hammonia Baltica (2,798 TEU, May 2011, Nordic Yards Warnemunde) sold to Hai An Transport and Stevedoring for USD 30.5 mil Inc TC, VV Value USD 28 mil.
