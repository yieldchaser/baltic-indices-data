Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, April 14 2026

Hellenic Shipping News  |  15 April 2026  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-april-14-2026

Bulkers:Bulker sale and purchase activity was healthy this week, with ten vessels transacting across the Kamsarmax,Panamax, Supramax, and Handysize segments, reflecting continued buyer appetite across the size spectrum. Evidence of soft pricing among mid-age and vintage Panamax and Handysize vessels resulted in dips to our values.

Panamax Xin Dong Guan 13 (76,100 DWT, 2012, Hudong) sold by Dongguan Haichang for USD 15.2 mil, VV Value USD 15.6 mil

Supramax Mercury Ocean (53,500 DWT, 2008, Iwagi Zosen) sold SS/DD Due to Chinese buyers for USD 13 mil, VV Value USD 13.3 mil

Handysizes COSCO Kunlunshan (32,000 DWT, 2010, Fujian Mawei) and Jin Wang Ling (31,800 DWT, 2010, Guangzhou Huangpu) sold in an enbloc deal by COSCO Shipping Bulk for USD 16.4 mil, VV Value USD 18.3 mil

Tankers:Despite the fluctuation in earnings this week, Tanker values held their firming trend across all segments. S&P activity has slowed down in the past number of weeks with only midsize/larger tonnage reported this week.

Suezmax Sienna & Cap Felix (150,200 & 158,800 DWT, 2007-2008, Universal/Samsung) sold by CMB Tech for USD 95 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 96.44 mil

Aframax Asia Ascend (115,400 DWT, Jul 2004, Samsung) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 32 mil, VV Value USD 30.52 mil

Containers:Second-hand values remain stable this week across all size segments. Handy container newbuilding values have picked up a little this week after a couple of new orders have been announced.

Feedermax He Sheng (1,096 TEU, Jul 2002, Santierul Naval Constantza) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 5.5 mil, VV Value USD 5.8 mil.

Sub Panamax Container Monaco (2,824 TEU, Nov 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to an undisclosed buyer for USD 25.2 mil, VV Value USD 25.2 mil.

Panamax Container Celsius Naples (4,944 TEU, Apr 2009, Daewoo) sold to Greek buyers for USD 34.5 mil Inc TC, VV Value USD 40.6 mil.
