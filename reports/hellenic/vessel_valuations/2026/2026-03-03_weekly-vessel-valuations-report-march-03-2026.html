Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, March 03 2026

in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 03/03/2026

Bulkers: Evidence of very strong pricing for modern Panamax tonnage, including the sale of the Three Saskias for USD 27.1 mil, has led to a substantial firming of values. Modern Supramax tonnage has also continued to show strong pricing. Softer pricing for Capesize newbuild orders and resales contributed to a dip in our values.

Newcastlemax Cape Kensington (203,700 DWT, Dec 2006, CSBC) sold by Cape Shipping SA for USD 26 mil, VV Value USD 26.5 mil

Kamsarmax Three Saskias (81,100 DWT, Mar 2014, Japan Marine United) sold by Tokei Kaiun for USD 27.1 mil, VV Value USD 25.0 mil

Ultramax Only You (60,500 DWT, Jan 2017, Sanoyas) sold by Alpha Omega Marine for USD 28.3 mil, VV Value USD 27.3 mil

Tankers: Secondhand activity ramped up this week across all sectors as we see values continuing to firm, further inflated by the recent boost in earnings

VLCC Shaybah (319,400 DWT, Jun 2008, Daewoo) sold DD Due by Bahri to Sinokor for USD 59 mil, VV Value USD 62.29 mil

VLCC Singapore Spirit (318,700 DWT, Jun 2013, Jiangnan Shangai Changxing HI) sold by Teekay Tankers to Sinokor for USD 84.5 mil, VV Value USD 86.59 mil

VLCC Eagle Vancouver (311,900 DWT, Jan 2013, Daewoo) sold DD Due by AET to Sinokor for USD 86.5 mil, VV Value USD 91.15 mil

VLCC Nave Galactic & Nave Buena Suerte (297,500 DWT, Mar 2011, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold by Navios Maritime Partners LP for USD 136.5 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 135.4 mil

Suezmax Stena Sunshine (159,000 DWT, Jan 2013, Samsung) sold by Stena Bulk for USD 62.5 mil, VV Value USD 62.18 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Hansa Tromsoe (51,500 DWT, Jul 2008, STX Offshore) sold DD Due by Leonhardt & Blumberg Shipmanagement for USD 16.9 mil, VV Value USD 17.81 mil

MR2 Romance (50,100 DWT, Jan 2009, SLS) sold by Koso Shipping for USD 15.8 mil, VV Value USD 16.04 mil

Containers: Midage Handy Containers are up a little this week following a couple of recent sales in the sector. Modern New Panamax values have fallen slightly

Panamax Container Seaspan Jakata (4,250 TEU, Feb 2006, Samsung) sold to Greek buyers for USD 17 mil Inc TC until Jan 2027, VV Value USD 27.4 mil.

Handy Container Hansa Fresenburg (1,740 TEU, Feb 2013, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to Global Feeder Shipping for USD 24.5 mil, VV Value USD 22.2 mil.

Feedermax Spirit of Dubai (1,155 TEU, Jul 2009, Sedef) sold forward delivery to Turkish buyers for USD 13 mil, VV Value USD 17.1 mil
