Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, January 13 2026

in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 14/01/2026

Bulkers: S&P activity has been low in the opening weeks of 2026 as Japan and parts of Europe observed their national holidays last week. Nonetheless, larger tonnage demonstrated soft pricing, leading to a dip in values.

Capesize Golden Magnum (179,800 DWT, 2009, Daewoo) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 28.7 mil, VV Value USD 29.6 mil

Kamsarmax Century Shanghai (82,000 DWT, 2018, Chengxi Shipyard) sold at auction for USD 25.0 mil, VV Value USD 27.5 mil

Tankers: VV VLCC Tanker values have firmed this week as a result of Sinokor’s recent buying spree, setting a new benchmark for secondhand prices.

8 x VLCC TBN (c.300k DWT, 2015-2016) sold to Sinokor by Frontline for USD 831.5 mil en bloc

6 x VLCC (300-306k DWT, 2007-2016) sold to Sinokor by CMB Tech for USD 520 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 504.04 mil

2 x VLCC Solana & Desimi (296k DWT, 2010-2011, Jiangnan Shanghai Changxing HI) sold to Sinokor by TMS Tankers for USD 136 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 130.51 mil

2 x VLCC Atlantas & Achilleas (298-321k DWT, Jun 2010, Daewoo & Universal) sold to Sinokor by Capital Ship Management for USD 140 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 136.85 mil

VLCC Oceanis (320,800 DWT, Apr 2011, Samsung) sold to Sinokor by Chandris for USD 68 mil, VV Value USD 68.99 mil

VLCC Nissos Psara (301,900 DWT, Feb 2011, IHI) sold to Sinokor by Kyklades Maritime for USD 68 mil, VV Value USD 69.22 mil

VLCC Achilleas (298,300 DWT, Jun 2010, Universal) sold to Sinokor by Capital Ship Management for USD 70 mil, VV Value USD 66.74 mil

VLCC Advantage Value (297,600 DWT, Jan 2009, Jiangnan Shanghai Changxing HI) sold to Sinokor by Advantage Tankers for USD 56 mil, VV Value USD 54.52 mi

Containers: Second-hand sales remain pretty scarce, and values largely remain stable. However, the appetite for newbuilding orders is still high with a number of new orders announced last week

Handy Container Boat Blessing (1,684 TEU, Stocznia Szczecin Nowa, Nov 1997) sold by undisclosed buyers for USD 8.6 mil DD Due, VV Value USD 9.2 mil
