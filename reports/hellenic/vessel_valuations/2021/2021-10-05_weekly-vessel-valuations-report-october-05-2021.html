Hellenic Shipping News  |  05 October 2021  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-05-2021

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report05/10/2021

Tanker:Modern LR1 values have firmed.

Suezmax Nordic Sirius (150,200 DWT, Oct 2000, NKK) sold to Far Eastern buyers for USD 14.00 mil, VV value USD 13.99 mil – BWTS Fitted.

MR2 Stena Conqueror (47,300 DWT, Oct 2003, Uljanik Brodogradiliste) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 6.90 mil, VV value USD 7.78 mil – DD Due.

Bulker:Panamax values have firmed.

Capesize Conrad (207,600 DWT, Apr 2017, Shanghai Waigaoqiao Shipbuilding) sold to Global Meridian Holdings for USD 55.00 mil, VV value USD 58.50 mil – Scrubber and BWTS fitted.

Capesize Aqua Honor (175,400 DWT, May 2012, Jinhai Intelligent Manufacturing) sold for USD 28.00 mil, VV value USD 27.62 mil – BWTS fitted.

Supramaxes Stove Friend and Stove Tide (57,700 DWT, Sep & Oct 2016, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to Belships in an en bloc deal for USD 56.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 57.18 mil – BWTS fitted.

Supramax Seacon 6 (56,800 DWT, Jun 2012, Zhejiang Zengzhou) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 15.30 mil, VV value USD 16.95 mil – At Auction.

Supramax Medi Okinawa (56,100 DWT, Dec 2011, Mitsui Tamano) sold to Bangladeshi buyers for USD 22.00 mil, VV value USD 21.88 mil – BWTS fitted.

Handymax Spring Sunshine (47,000 DWT, Mar 2013, Jiangsu New Yangzijiang) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 17.90 mil, VV value USD 16.84 mil – BWTS fitted.

Handy Bulkers New History and New Inspiration (36,300 DWT, Mar & May 2013, Shikoku Dockyard) sold to Tufton Oceanic Assests in and en bloc deal for USD 41.20 mil, VV en bloc value USD 43.32 mil.

Container:Container values have remained stable.

Sub Panamax Atlantic Flosta (2,474 TEU, May 2002, Volkswerft) sold to MSC for USD 29.00 mil, VV value USD 30.31 mil.

Handy Filia T (1,774 TEU, Jan 2019, Jiangsu New Yangzijiang) sold to Wan Hai Lines for USD 41.00 mil, VV value USD 42.74 mil – BWTS fitted.
