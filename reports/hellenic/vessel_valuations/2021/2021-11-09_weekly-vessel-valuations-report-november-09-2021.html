inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report09/11/2021

Tanker:Tanker values have remained stable.

Aframax Atalandi (105,300 DWT, Nov 2004, Daewoo) sold for USD 13.60 mil, VV value USD 14.16 mil – BWTS fitted.

LR1 Iris Victoria (75,000 DWT, Jun 2010, Minami Nippon) sold to Transocean for USD 17.35 mil, VV value USD 16.78 mil – BWTS fitted.

MR2 Star Eagle (51,200 DWT, Oct 2007, STX Offshore) sold for USD 11.75 mil, VV value USD 12.39 mil.

MR2 Angel 61 (48,700 DWT, May 2006, Iwagi Zosen) sold to Greek buyers for USD 9.80 mil, VV value USD 9.58 mil – SS/DD Passed, BWTS fitted.

Bulker:Bulker values have softened.

Panamax Hull 20152686 (82,300 DWT, Jan 2022, Jiangsu Yangzi-Mitsui) sold to Greek buyers for USD 38.50 mil, VV value USD 39.24 mil – Resale.

Handy Bulker Ortolan Beta Strait (30,000 DWT, May 2010, Yangzhou Guoyu Shipbuilding) sold for USD 11.75 mil, VV value USD 12.84 mil.

Container:Panamax values have softened.

No sales to report.
