inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report13/07/2021

Tanker:Tanker values have remained stable.

VLCC Olympic Legend (309,300 DWT, Sep 2003, Samsung) sold to UAE buyers for USD 31.00 mil, VV value USD 29.31 mil. – DD Due.

LR2s Lion and Hull 170 (114,000 DWT, Aug 2021, Hanjin Subic) sold to USA buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 110.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 106.94 mil – Resale.

MR1 Adara (37,000 DWT, Apr 2011, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Leon Shipping and Trading for USD 15.75 mil, VV value USD 14.70 mil – SS Passed.

Post Panamax Double Providence (95,700 DWT, Jan 2012, Imabari) sold to Greek buyers for USD 21.25 mil, VV value USD 23.20 mil – BWTS on order.

Panamax Sea Express (79,300 DWT, Jul 2012, COSCO Dalian) sold to Greek buyers for USD 18.70 mil, VV value USD 18.40 mil.

Panamax QC Matilde (76,000 DWT, Jan 2002, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold for USD 10.80 mil, VV value USD 10.84 mil.

Supramax Bulk Costa Rica (58,800 DWT, Jun 2012, Nantong COSCO KHI) sold to Greek buyers for USD 18.60 mil, VV value USD 19.04 mil – BWTS fitted.

Supramax Bulk Patagonia (58,700 DWT, Mar 2012, Kawasaki), Bulk Curacao and Bulk Ecuador (57,900 DWT, May & Sep 2011, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to Greek buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 56.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 57.00 mil.

Supramax Centenario Forza (56,100 DWT, Apr 2012, Mitsui Tamano) sold for USD 18.50 mil, VV value USD 19.45 mil.

Supramax Belfri (55,900 DWT, Jun 2007, Kawasaki) sold to Jinhui Shipping and Transportation for USD 15.18 mil, VV value USD 14.84 mil.

Handy Bulker Dry Beam Neo (38,200 DWT, Dec 2019, Shin Kochi) sold to Nova Marine Carriers for USD 26.00 mil, VV value USD 28.14 mil – BWTS fitted.
