Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, December 14 2021

Hellenic Shipping News  |  14 December 2021  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-december-14-2021

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report14/12/2021

Tanker:Tanker values have softened.

VLCC Asian Progress III (306,400 DWT, Aug 2004, Mitsubishi HI) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 27.20 mil, VV value USD 27.96 mil – SS/DD Due.

MR2 High Valor (47,000 DWT, Feb 2005, STX Offshore) sold for USD 10.30 mil, VV value USD 9.62 mil – BWTS Fitted.

Bulker:Supramax and Handysize values have firmed.

Panamax Egyptian Mike and George P (81,600 DWT, Sept 2011 – Aug 2012, Longxue) sold in an en bloc deal to Costamare for USD 39.00 mil, VV value USD 43.10 mil – SS/DD Passed.

Panamax Silver Star (79,200 DWT, Jun 2011, COSCO Dalian) sold to Greek buyers for USD 18.00 mil, VV value USD 18.92 mil – SS/DD passed.

Supramax Union Erwin (55,700 DWT, Apr 2011, IHI) sold to German buyers for USD 21.00 mil, VV value USD 20.53 mil – SS/DD passed.

Handymax Aquata (46,700 DWT, Jan 1999, Sanoyas) sold for USD 10.30 mil, VV value USD 9.23 mil – SS/DD Due, BWTS fitted.

Handy Bulker Targa (28,400 DWT, May 2009, Imabari) sold to European buyers for USD 14.10 mil, VV value USD 13.29 mil – DD due, BWTS fitted.

Container:Container values have softened.

Panamax COSCO Kawasaki (4,334 TEU, Jul 2010, Samsung HI) sold for USD 65.00 mil, VV value USD 67.27 mil.

Sub Panamax Atlantic Solli (2,007 TEU, Mar 2008, Zhejiang Shipbuilding) sold for USD 26.00 mil, VV value USD 27.46 mil.
