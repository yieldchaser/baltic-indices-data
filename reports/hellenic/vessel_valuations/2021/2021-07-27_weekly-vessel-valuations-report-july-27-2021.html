Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, July 27 2021

Hellenic Shipping News  |  27 July 2021  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-july-27-2021

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report27/07/2021

Tanker:Aframax values have firmed slightly.

Small Chemical Tankers Beech Galaxy & Lime Galaxy (20,000 DWT, Jan 2007 & Dec 2008, Usuki Zosensho) sold in an en bloc deal to USA buyers for USD 23.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 24.75 mil

Small Chemical Tanker Celsius Manhattan (19,800 DWT, Oct 2006, Kitanihon Zosen) sold to Taihua Ship Management for USD 11.50 mil, VV value USD 10.38 mil – SS Due.

Bulker:Softening in modern Supramax values.

Panamax Xing Bao (72,100 DWT, Sept 1997, Hitachi) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 8.30 mil, VV value USD 7.48 mil – DD passed.

Ultramax Nord Hudson (61,600 DWT, Aug 2014, Nantong COSCO KHI) sold to European buyers for USD 24.40 mil, VV value USD 25.22 mil.

Ultramax New Aspiration (61,400 DWT, Oct 2011, Iwagi Zosen) sold to Greek buyers for USD 21.50 mil, VV value USD 22.18 mil – BWTS fitted.

Supramax Nord Trust (55,700 DWT, Nov 2009, Mitsui Tamano) sold for USD 16.00 mil, VV value USD 16.97 mil.

Handy Bulker Nordic Incheon (35,800 DWT, Jan 2018, Samjin Shipbuilding) sold for USD 20.50 mil, VV value USD 19.04 mil.

Handy Bulker Geki Star (28,400 DWT, Jul 2008, Shimanami) sold for USD 10.30 mil, VV value USD 10.49 mil.

Container:Container values have firmed.

No sales with disclosed prices have been confirmed this week.
