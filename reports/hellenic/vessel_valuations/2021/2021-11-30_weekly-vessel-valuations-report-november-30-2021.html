Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, November 30 2021

Hellenic Shipping News  |  30 November 2021  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-november-30-2021

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report30/11/2021

Tanker:Tanker values have remained stable.

MR2 Arctic Bay (50,900 DWT, Mar 2006, STX Offshore) sold for USD 11.50 mil, VV value USD 10.89 mil.

Small Clean Tanker Maria Theresa (4,500 DWT, Mar 2002, RMK Marine) sold to UAE buyers for USD 2.00 mil, VV value USD 2.85 mil – SS/DD Due.

Small Clean Tanker Else Marie Theresa (4,300 DWT, Dec 2001, Gisan) sold to UAE buyers for USD 2.00 mil, VV value USD 2.73 mil – SS/DD Due.

Bulker:Handy Bulker values have firmed.

Capesize Hull 503 (181,500 DWT, Mar 2022, Namura) sold to Ocean Longevity for USD 59.00 mil, VV value USD 57.50 mil – Resale, BWTS fitted.

Post Panamax Mayfair Spirit (93,300 DWT, May 2011, Jiangsu New Yangzijiang) sold for USD 19.00 mil, VV value USD 19.50 mil – SS/DD Passed, BWTS fitted.

Panamaxes MSXT Echo, Hull 832, Hull 833 and Hull 834 (85,000 DWT, Dec 2021 – Sep 2022, Chengxi Shipyard) sold to Singaporean buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 130.00 mil, VV value 135.84 mil – Resale, BWTS Fitted.

Handy Bulker New Days (38,200 DWT, Jan 2017, Shin Kochi) sold for USD 27.00 mil, VV value USD 25.19 mil – SS/DD Due, BWTS fitted.

Handy Bulker Royal Justice (37,000 DWT, Dec 2012, Saiki) sold to Fortius Ship Management for USD 19.50 mil, VV value USD 19.39 mil – At Auction, BWTS fitted.

Container:Container values have softened due to earnings.

No Container sales to report this week.
