Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 03 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  03 June 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-03-2025

Bulkers: Bulker values have remained stable this week, with a small increase in older tonnage Supramaxesdue to sales such as Swan River.

Kamsarmax BC Key Action (82,200 DWT, Apr 2010, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold SS/DD Passed to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 15.1 mil, VV Value USD 14.96 mil

Panamax BC Jawor (79,700 DWT, Jun 2010, New Times Shipbuilding) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 11 mil, VV Value USD 12.3 mil

Supramax BC Swan River (56,000 DWT, May 2005, Mitsui Tamano) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 10 mil, VV Value USD 9.2 mil

Supramax BC Ivy Alliance (55,900 DWT, Jan 2011, IHI) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 15.6 mil, VV Value USD 15.53 mil

Handy BC Maple Tulip (33,200 DWT, Jan 2011, Kanda) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.8 mil, VV Value USD 11.85 mil

Tankers:Continued softening of values for older and smaller tonnage this week following more sales, values for larger tonnage remains stable.

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Pelagic Tarpon (50,900 DWT, May 2006, STX Offshore) sold to Ocean Beauty Seafood for USD 14 mil, VV Value USD 13.7 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) STI Regina (50,000 DWT, Sep 2014, SPP) sold to Great Eastern Shipping for USD 28 mil, VV Value USD 29.6 mil

Small Clean (Chemical/Product) Ding Heng 2 (4,000 DWT, Jun 2007, Zhoushan Dingheng) sold to Middle Eastern buyers for USD 4 mil, VV Value USD 3.9 mil

Containers:Recent Handysize and Sub Panamaxes sales firming the values of modern vessels this week. Older Feedermaxes also continue to firm.

Sub Panamax Cape Quest (2,190 TEU, Mar 2017, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to CMA CGM for USD 35 mil, VV Value USD 32.4 mil.

Handysize H Mercury (1,800 TEU, Apr 2022, Jiangsu Yangzi Xinfu) sold for USD 34.5 mil, VV Value USD 34.3 mil.
