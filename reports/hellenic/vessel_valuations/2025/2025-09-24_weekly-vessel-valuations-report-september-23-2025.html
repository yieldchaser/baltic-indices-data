inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report24/09/2025

Bulkers:VV Bulker values have remained relatively stable with a small increase for Panamaxes and Supramaxesdue to sales such as Sunshine Bliss.

Panamax BC Sunshine Bliss (76,400 DWT, Feb 2010, Oshima) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 15.9 mil, VV Value USD 14.86 mil

Ultramax BC DSI Drammen (63,400 DWT, Aug 2016, I-S) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 25.91 mil

Handy BC Global Mermaid (33,700 DWT, Jan 2010, Shin Kochi) sold DD Passed to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 10.9 mil, VV Value USD 11.2 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remain mostly stable this week while older tonnage VLCC values soften owing to the sale of FPMC C Knight, despite the recent uptick in earnings

VLCC FPMC C Knight (301,900 DWT, Feb 2011, IHI) sold to Kyklades Maritime for USD 55 mil, VV Value USD 56.91 mil

Suezmax Jag Lok (158,300 DWT, Mar 2005, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) on subs to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 26 mil, VV Value USD 27.86 mil

Aframax Patmos Warrior (105,600 DWT, Oct 2007, Sumitomo) sold DD Due to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 28 mil, VV Value USD 28.27 mil

Containers:Limited movement across the whole sector last week

Post Panamax Hulls 1140089, 114091, 114106 and 114118 (8,850 TEU, Feb 2028, Hanjin HI) sold for USD 460 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 473 mil
