Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 13 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  13 May 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-13-2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report13/05/2025

Bulkers:Bulker values have remained stable with older Handysize down this week mainly owing to recent deals such as CS Vanguard

Capesize BC Mineral China (171,100 DWT, Nov 2003, Hyundai Heavy Ind Ulsan Shipyard) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 13 mil, VV Value USD 14.37 mil

Supramax BC Aquavita Lime (63,700 DWT, Jan 2021, Nantong Xiangyu) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 30.5 mil, VV Value USD 30.32 mil

Handy BC Resource (31,800 DWT, May 2010, Guangzhou Huangpu Shipbuilding) sold SS/ DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.7 mil, VV Value USD 8.74 mil

Handy BC CS Vanguard (26,500 DWT, Aug 2007, Sungdong) sold DD Due to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 6.5 mil, VV Value USD 7.09 mil

Tankers:Tanker values have seen some fluctuation over the last week mainly owing to recent deals

VLCC Pacific Loyalty (307,300 DWT, Dec 2006, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold DD Due to Unknown Chinese for USD 42 mil, VV Value USD 41.69 mil

MR1 (Chemical/Product) Zagara (37,300 DWT, Apr 2002, STX Offshore) sold DD Due to Unknown Nigerian buyers for USD 8 mil, VV Value USD 8.29 mil

Containers:Newbuild and older Sub Panamax values have firmed this week on the back of recent sales and orders while Post Panamax newbuild values have softened

Sub Panamax Jeju Island (2,741 TEU, Feb 2006, Aker Ostsee) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 20.5 mil, VV Value USD 19.23 mil.

Sub Panamax TB Kaiyuan (2,432 TEU, Dec 1997, Daewoo) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 13.3 mil, VV Value USD 13.35 mil.

Feedermax Rachel Borchard (1,216 TEU, Jun 2000, Hanjin Heavy Ind) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 6.8 mil, VV Value USD 5.68 mil.
