Bulkers:Bulker values have remained stable for larger tonnage this week, with a decrease in smaller tonnageSupramaxes and Handysize due to sales such as Pythias and NY Trader III

Kamsarmax BC Explorer Oceania (81,100 DWT, Dec 2015, Jiangsu Hantong Ship Heavy Industry) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 20.4 mil, VV Value USD 20.37 mil

Supramax BC Pythias (58,000 DWT, May 2010, Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding) sold SS/DD Due to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.2 mil, VV Value USD 11.36 mil

Handy BC NY Trader III (39,300 DWT, Sep 2016, Jiangmen Nanyang) sold to unknown European buyers for USD 17.3 mil, VV Value USD 17.87 mil

Handy BC Aries Sakura (39,900 DWT, Jun 2020, Shin Kurushima Toyohashi) sold SS/DD Passed to unknown Greek buyers for USD 25.9 mil, VV Value USD 26.16 mil
