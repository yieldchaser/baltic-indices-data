Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, March 18 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 March 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-march-18-2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report19/03/2025

Bulkers:Bulker Values firming across the board this week, owing to improved freight ratesand sales of vessels such as Kmarin Oslo and Achilles Bulker

Capesize BC Mount Song (180,200 DWT, Mar 2010, Koyo Dock) sold SS/DD due to Far Eastern buyers for USD 27 mil, VV Value USD 27.57 mil.

Kamsarmax BC Energy Hope (82,100 DWT, Feb 2012, Tsuneishi Zosen) sold DD Passed to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 17.79 mil

Handy BC Achilles Bulker (32,700 DWT, Oct 2003, Kanda) sold to Lebanese buyers for USD 6.6 mil, VV Value USD 6.22 mil

Handy BC (Open Hatch) Seastar Merlin (39,800 DWT, May 2025, Naikai) sold to South Korean buyers for USD 34 mil, VV Value USD 34.13 mil

Tankers:Tanker values have remained stable on a negative trend over the last week with a number of smaller, clean vessels sold to unknown Chinese buyers.

VLCC Australis (299,100 DWT, Nov 2003, Universal) sold for USD 28 mil to SEACON trading as an FSU w/out BWTS, VV value USD 33.14 mil.

Teekay sold Suezmax Tianlong Spirit and LR2 Galway Spirit (159,000 DWT & 105,200 DWT, Jan 2009 & Jan 2007, Bohai Shipbuilding & Hyundai Heavy Ulsan) in an en bloc deal for USD 59 mil, VV enbloc value USD 61.28 mil.

MR2 Torm Resilience and Ragnhild (50,000 DWT & 46,200 DWT, 2005, STX Offshore) sold to Chinese buyers in an enbloc deal for USD 30 mil, VV enbloc value USD 30.3 mil respectively.

MR2 Torm Thames (47,000 DWT, Jul 2005, Hyundai Mipo) sold to unknown Chinese for USD 15 mil SS/DD due, VV value USD 15.68 mil.

Containers:Stability continues for the larger sectors this week, whilst mid-age Panamaxes increased and smaller vessels crept up too.

Handy Containers AS Fabiana and AS Franziska (1,296 TEU & 1,345 TEU, Oct 2007 & Jun 2005, Zhejiang Ouhua & Jiangsu Yangzijiang) sold to European buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 21 mil, VV en bloc value USD 22.1 mil.

Sub Panamax Haris (2,194 TEU, Nov 2015, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to Neptune Pacific Direct Line for USD 31 mil, VV Value USD 30.68 mil.
