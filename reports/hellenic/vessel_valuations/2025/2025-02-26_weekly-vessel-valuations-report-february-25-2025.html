Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 25 2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report26/02/2025

Bulkers:Mid age values down slightly on last week, whilst newbuild values for larger tonnage firmed

Capesize BC (Newcastlemax) HL Frontier (208,000 DWT, May 2010, Universal) Sold SS/DD due to undisclosed buyers for USD 32.5 mil, VV Value USD 29.7 mil

Panamax BC Sentosa Spirit (81,900 DWT, Feb 2020, Tsuneishi Zosen) Sold SS/DD due to unknown Greek buyers for USD 32 mil, VV Value USD 33.33 mil

Supramax BC Sagar Shakti (58,100 DWT, Apr 2012, Tsuneishi Zhoushan) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.9 mil, VV Value USD 15.41 mil

Supramax BC Spar Lynx (53,200 DWT, Mar 2005, Chengxi Shipyard) sold SS/DD due to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 8 mil, VV Value USD 7.41 mil

Tankers:VV Tanker values remain mostly stable this week with older tonnage VLCC firming slightly owing to the recent sale Great Lady

VLCC Great Lady (308,900 DWT, Jul 2005, Samsung) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 41 mil, VV Value USD 38.08 mil

Suezmax Front Brage (156,000 DWT, Mar 2011, Jiangsu Rongsheng) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 41 mil, VV Value USD 40.11 mil

Containers:Values remain stable across the board this week, with only small movements for older larger vessels.

4 x Post Panamax CMA CGM Africa One, Two, Three and Four (3,718 TEU, Aug-Oct 2010, Hanjin Subic) sold to CMA CGM for USD 160 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 155.35 mil

Handysize Aydogan (1,730 TEU, Nov 1999, Stocznia Szczecin Nowa) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 8 mil, VV Value USD 9.2 mil
