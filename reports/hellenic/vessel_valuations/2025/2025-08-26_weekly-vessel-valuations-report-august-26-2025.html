Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, August 26 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  26 August 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-august-26-2025

Bulkers

Values have mostly firmed this week, with mid age tonnage seeing the biggest increase owing to second-hand sales

Supramax BC Endeavor (53,500 DWT, Apr 2008, Nam Trieu) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 9 mil, VV Value USD 8.35 mil

Handy BC Bobic (31,900 DWT, Jan 2006, Hakodate Dock) sold to unknown Turkish buyers for USD 7.8 mil, VV Value USD 8.03 mil

Tankers

Tanker values remained stable this week as S&P markets stayed relatively quiet

VLCC Searacer (297,300 DWT, Apr 2009, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 48 mil, VV Value USD 47.86 mil

Suezmax Front Brage (156,000 DWT, Mar 2011, Jiangsu Rongsheng) sold to Global Marine Services for USD 38 mil, VV Value USD 37.99 mil

Containers

Midage Handy and Sub Panamax Container values continue to rise as demand for second-hand vessels stays firm

Handy Container Honrise (1,662 TEU, Jul 2001, Shin Kurushima Onishi) sold to FESCO for USD 13 mil, VV Value USD 12.7 mil

Handy Container Easline Dalian (1,675 TEU, Oct 1998, Kanasashi) sold to Unknown buyers for USD 10 mil, VV Value USD 10.5 mil
