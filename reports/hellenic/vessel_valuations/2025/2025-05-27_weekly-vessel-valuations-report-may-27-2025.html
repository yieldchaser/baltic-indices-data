Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 27 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  27 May 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-27-2025

Bulkers:Bulker values have remained stable this week, with a small decrease in older tonnage Capesizesdue to sales such as Tradership and Thalassini Agatha.

Capesize BC Tradership (176,900 DWT, Oct 2006, Namura) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 19 mil, VV Value USD 19.3 mil

Capesize BC Thalassini Agatha (182,300 DWT, Jan 2011, Universal) sold DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 28.8 mil, VV Value USD 30.22 mil

Panamax BC Kazahaya (81,800 DWT, Mar 2017, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold DD Passed to unknown buyers for USD 27.5 mil, VV Value USD 26.3 mil

Supramax BC NZ Hangzhou (56,700 DWT, Jun 2012, Qingshan Shipyard) sold DD Due to Hai Dong International Shipping for USD 12 mil, VV Value USD 12.49 mil

Handy BC Amstel Confidence (38,500 DWT, Nov 2011, Minami Nippon) sold DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.3 mil, VV Value USD 14.16 mil

Tankers:Older and smaller tonnage Tanker values have softened over the last week owing to recent deals while we mainly see stability for large tonnage values

VLCC M Star (314,000 DWT, Dec 2008, Kawasaki) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 46.75 mil, VV Value USD 51.18 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Seaways Citron & Seaways Frontier (50,000 DWT, 2007, Hyundai Mipo) sold in an en bloc deal to undisclosed buyers for USD 28 mil, VV Value USD 32.25 mil

Containers:Older Feedermaxes are continuing to firm whilst modern vessels remain fairly stable

Feedermax Panda Vega (906 TEU, Feb 2006, Hakata Zosen) sold for USD 9.9 mil, VV Value USD 8.9 mil

Sub Panamax ST Success (2,553 TEU, Jul 2010, Naikai Setoda) sold for USD 27 mil, VV Value USD 26.3 mil
