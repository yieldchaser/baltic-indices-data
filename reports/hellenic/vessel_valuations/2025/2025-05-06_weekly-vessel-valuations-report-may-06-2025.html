Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, May 06 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  06 May 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-may-06-2025

Bulkers:Bulker values have remained stable with older Supramax down this week mainly owing to recent deals such as Spar Capella

Panamax BC Ivestos 6 (76,600 DWT, Apr 2006, Imabari) sold DD Due to Unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 9 mil, VV Value USD 10.12 mil

Supramax BC Spar Capella (58,000 DWT, Jan 2011, Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 14.3 mil, VV Value USD 12.79 mil

Handy BC (Open Hatch) IVS Knot (33,100 DWT, Aug 2010, Kanda) sold SS/DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.5 mil, VV Value USD 11.71 mil

Tankers:Tanker values have seen some fluctuation over the last week mainly owing to recent deals

VLCC Layla (317,800 DWT, Aug 2007, Hyundai Samho HI) on subs to Unknown Chinese for USD 46.5 mil, VV Value USD 45.36 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Gulf Elan (46,900 DWT, Jan 2007, Hyundai Mipo) sold DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 16 mil, VV Value USD 16.41 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Tamiat Navigator (46,600 DWT, Aug 2010, Hyundai Mipo) sold SS/DD Due to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 18 mil, VV Value USD 20.77 mil

6 x MR2 (Chemical/Product) (46,000 DWT, 2016-2017, Hyundai Mipo) sold in an en bloc deal to Tsakos Energy Navigation for USD 192 mil, VV Value USD 196.9 mil

Containers:Newbuild Sub Panamax and older Feedermaxes are up this week on the back of recent sales.

Handy Container Mindoro (1,781 TEU, Dec 2022, Huanghai Shipyards) sold for USD 31.5 mil inc TC, VV Value USD 34 mil.

5/9 x ULCV (18,500 TEU, 2028/29, NACKS/DACKS) ordered for USD 220 mil, VV Value USD 231 mil.
