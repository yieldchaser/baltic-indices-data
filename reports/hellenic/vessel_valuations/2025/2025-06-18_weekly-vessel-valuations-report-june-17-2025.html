Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, June 17 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 June 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-17-2025

Bulkers:Bulker values have remained stable this week, with a small decrease in younger Supramxesdue to sales such as CMB Rubens

Supramax BC CMB Rubens (63,500 DWT, Jul 2018, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 27.3 mil, VV Value USD 29.51 mil

Handy BC Darya Ganga (36,800 DWT, Feb 2012, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.3 mil, VV Value USD 13.68 mil

Tankers:Tanker values have fluctuated this week owing to some recent secondhand deals and orders. S&P markets were once again focused on the MR segment, with values and earnings largely holding steady after consistent downward pressure in recent weeks.

MR2 (Chemical/Product) UOG Constantine G (50,000 DWT, Mar 2010, SLS) sold SS/DD Due to Greek buyers for USD 17.1 mil, VV Value USD 18.6 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) UOG Despina V (50,000 DWT, Jul 2010, SLS) sold SS/DD Due to Greek buyers for USD 17.1 mil, VV Value USD 19 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) UOG Phoenix (47,400 DWT, Apr 2010, Onomichi Dockyard) sold SS/DD Due to Greek buyers for USD 16.8 mil, VV Value USD 16.68 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Glenda Melissa (47,200 DWT, Feb 2011, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 19 mil, VV Value USD 20.39 mil

Containers:Modern Sub Panamax vessels are nudging up and older ones down following recent sales, older Post Panamaxes continue to make small gains

Feedermax SITC Yokohama (831 TEU, Aug 2004, Kyokuyo) sold for USD 7.25 mil, VV Values USD 7.2 mil
