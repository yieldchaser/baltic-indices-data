Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, November 04 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  05 November 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-november-04-2025

Bulkers:VV Bulker values have mostly remained stable this week, with older Supramaxes seeing a decrease

Capesize BC Mineral Subic (179,400 DWT, May 2011, Hanjin Subic) sold to Agricore Shipping for USD 30.25 mil, VV Value USD 30.01

Ultramax BC CMB Bruegel (63,700 DWT, Feb 2021, Shin Kasado Dock) sold to unknown Bangladeshi buyers for USD 32.5 mil, VV Value USD 32.9 mil

Supramax BC KN Future (58,000 DWT, Oct 2013, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 18.75 mil, VV Value USD 17.89 mil

Handy BC Seacon Manila (33,400 DWT, Mar 2016, Shin Kochi) sold SS/DD due to Bulk Exports International for USD 17.1 mil, VV Value USD 16.53 mil

Tankers:Tanker S&P activity slowed slightly, with owners weighing strong spot earnings against the opportunity to offload ageing tonnage at attractive prices

VLCC Nave Quasar (297,400 DWT, Sep 2010, Dalian Shipbuilding Ind Corp) was sold by Navios Maritime Partners LP (SS/DD Passed) for USD 52.5 mil, VV Value USD 51.69 mil

LR1 Ryvingen Sun (74,000 DWT, May 2007, New Century) was sold by Sole Shipping (DD Passed) for USD 14.5 mil, VV Value USD 13.43 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Seaways Gatun & Seaways Huron (47,900 DWT, 2007, Hyundai Mipo) sold in an en bloc deal to Champion Tankers AS for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 28.3 mil

Containers:Older smaller tonnage has softened this week.

Handy Container Warnow Whale (1,296 TEU, Feb 2007, Zhejiang Ouhua) sold by Marlow Navigation for USD 13 mil Inc TC, VV Value USD 15.45 mil
