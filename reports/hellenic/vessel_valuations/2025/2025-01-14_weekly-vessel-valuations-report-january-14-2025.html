inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report14/01/2025

Bulkers:Bulker values remain stable this week

Panamax BC Alpha Melody (74,500 DWT, May 2002, Daewoo) sold DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 6.60 mil, VV Value USD 7.95 mil

Handymax BC Hibiscus (48,600 DWT, Jun 2002, Hakodate Dock) sold DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 7 mil, VV Value USD 7.2 mil

Supramax BC (Open Hatch) My Fair Lady (50,500 DWT, Mar 2011 Oshima) sold to unknown far Eastern buyers for 15 mil, VV Value USD 16.01 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remained mainly stable across the board last week with slight softening observed for MR values due to earnings continuing to decrease

Aframax Nissos Delos (115,700 DWT, Aug 2012, Samsung) sold to MJL Bangladesh for USD 45.3 mil, VV Value USD 42.82 mil

LR2 Suvretta (109,300 DWT, Oct 2008, STX Offshore) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 31 mil, VV Value USD 31.78 mil

Containers:Container values remaining stable last week with older tonnage Feedermax values softening due to recent Contship sales

Feedermax Contship Med (1,100 TEU, Dec 2004, Jinling Shipyard Jiangsu) sold to Medkon Lines for USD 7 mil, VV Value USD 7.24 mil

Feedermax Contship Leo (1,118 TEU, Oct 2008, Taizhou Kouan Shipbuilding) sold to Safesea Group for USD 10 mil, VV Value USD 10.43 mil
