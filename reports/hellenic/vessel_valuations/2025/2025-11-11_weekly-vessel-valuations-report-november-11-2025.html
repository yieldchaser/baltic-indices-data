inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report11/11/2025

Bulkers:VV Bulker values have mostly remained stable this week, with older Handysize seeing an increase dueto sales such Chamchuri Naree

Panamax BC Ornak (79,700 DWT, Sep 2010, New Times Shipbuilding) sold SS/DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.35 mil, VV Value USD 12.5 mil

Supramax BC Ince Ege (57,400 DWT, Apr 2010, STX Dalian) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.8 mil, VV Value USD 13.2 mil

Handy BC Chamchuri Naree (33,700 DWT, Jun 2005, Shin Kochi) sold SS/DD passed to Unknown Turkish buyers for USD 8.2 mil, VV Value USD 8.13 mil

Tankers:VV Tanker values remain stable this week as the S&P market remains active. However, this week Tanker newbuild orders surged, especially for VLCCs and LR2s.

Aframax Platanos (114,600 DWT, Sep 2019, Namura) sold to Tankerska Plovidba for USD 66.5 mil, VV Value USD 67 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Hellas Avatar (50,000 DWT, Sep 2015, Hyundai Mipo) sold to Euroholdings (SS/DD Passed) for USD 31.8 mil, VV Value USD 32.43 mil

MR2 Yosemite Trader (48,000 DWT, Mar 2011, Iwagi Zosen) sold to Unknown Vietnamese buyers (SS/DD Due) for USD 21.5 mil, VV Value USD 22.11 mil

MR1 (Chemical/Product) STI Brixton (38,700 DWT, Jun 2014, Hyundai Mipo) was sold by Scorpio Tankers for USD 30 mil, VV Value USD 29.36 mil

Containers:Smaller tonnage VV Container values continue to soften this week.

Panamax Newnew Star 2 (3,534 TEU, Dec 2007, Shanghai Shipyard) sold to MSC for USD 26 mil, VV Value USD 25.8 mil

Handy Container Panay (1,930 TEU, Jul 2023, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to CMA CGM for USD 35.5 mil, VV Value USD 36.2 mil
