Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, April 22 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  22 April 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-april-22-2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report22/04/2025

Bulkers: Bulker values have mostly firmed this week mainly owing to recent deals such as Sea Venus, Oakgate and Majestic Marina.

Panamax BC Sea Venus (80,900 DWT, Oct 2013, New Century) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.7 mil, VV Value USD 16.57 mil

Ultramax BC Oakgate & Maplegate (60,400-63,500 DWT, 2018-2019, Oshima) sold Enbloc to unknown Indonesian buyers for USD 63 mil, VV Value USD 62.34 mil

Handy BC Majestic Marina (32,100 DWT, Jul 2009, Hakodate Dock) sold DD Due to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 10.5 mil, VV Value USD 10.25 mil

Tankers:Tanker values have remained mostly stable across the board over the last week as uncertainty remains

Aframax P Sophia (105,100 DWT, Sep 2009, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold SS/DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 36.05 mil, VV Value USD 33.95 mil

Containers:Values remained fairly stable last week, midage and older Feedermaxes nudging up

Handy Container Torres Strait (1,713 TEU, Jul 2008, CSBC) sold to CMA CGM for USD 17.2 mil, del Aug, VV Value USD 18.9 mil

Handy Container Tasman Strait (1,713 TEU, May 2008, CSBC) sold to MSC for USD 17.2 mil, del Aug, VV Value USD 18.7 mil
