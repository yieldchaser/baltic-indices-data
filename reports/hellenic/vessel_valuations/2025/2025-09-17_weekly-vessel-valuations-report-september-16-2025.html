Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, September 16 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  17 September 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-september-16-2025

Bulkers:VV Bulker values have remained relatively stable despite the strong S&P activity across each bulker sector

Panamax BC Silver Navigator (80,300 DWT, Jul 2011, STX Offshore) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 15.5 mil, VV Value USD 16.12 mil

Ultramax BC Jal Kalpataru & Jal Kalpavriksh (66,300 DWT, 2021, Mitsui Tamano) sold in an en bloc deal to Precious Shipping for USD 65.1 mil, VV Value USD 67.23 mil Supramax BC Marinor (56,800 DWT, Jul 2009, Jiangsu Hantong Ship HI) sold DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 10.8 mil, VV Value USD 10.38 mil

Handy BC Stellar Aragvi (28,400 DWT, Jan 2014, Imabari) sold to Bahtera Adhiguna for USD 12.5 mil, VV Value USD 12.43 mil

Tankers:Tanker values remain mostly stable this week with older Suezmax values firming modestly based on some recent transactions

Suezmax Kriti Vigor (159,100 DWT, Feb 2005, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold SS/DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 29 mil, VV Value USD 28.73 mil

Suezmax Samurai (150,000 DWT, Feb 2009, Universal) sold to Unknown Indian buyers for USD 39 mil, VV Value USD 36.98 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Hafnia Lupus (52,600 DWT, Apr 2012, Guangzhou Shipyard International Company Limited) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 20 mil, VV Value USD 21.31 mil

Containers:Older Handy Containers are up and older Feedermaxes are down slightly following recent sales, other sectors remain stable

Handy Container Cape Franklin (1,440 TEU, Dec 2006, P+S Weften) sold for USD 17.5 mil, VV Value USD 14.67 mil

Feedermax JSP Carla (750 TEU, Dec 2004, Detlef Hegemann Rolandwerft) sold for USD 6.5 mil, VV Value USD 6.87 mil
