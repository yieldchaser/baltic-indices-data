Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, February 18 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 February 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-february-18-2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report18/02/2025

Bulkers: Mid age values down slightly on last week, whilst newbuild values for larger tonnage firmed

Capesize BC Cape Herono (177,700 DWT, Feb 2005, Mitsui Ichihara) Sold SS/DD due to unknown Chinese buyer for USD 14.10 mil, VV Value USD 16.37 mil

Panamax BC Sentosa Challenge (81.600 DWT, Apr 2020, Imabari) Sold SS/DD due to unknown Greek buyers for USD 32 mil, VV Value USD 32.58 mil

Supramax BC River Globe (53,600 DWT, Dec 2007, Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding) to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.55 mil, VV Value USD 9.4 mil

Handy BC Nord Namami (38,200 DWT, Jul 2012, Kanda) sold SS/DD due to undisclosed buyers for 13.5 mil, VV Value USD 14.55 mil

Tankers:A quieter week for the tanker sector as we see values remaining stable again

VLCC Loggam (309,200 DWT, Jan 2003, Samsung) sold to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 29.5 mil, VV Value USD 29.47 mil

LR2 Kavafis & Elytis (114,000 DWT, 2023-2024, SWS) sold en bloc to undisclosed buyers for USD 143.5 mil, VV Value USD 141.59 mil

Containers:Container values remain mostly stable across the board this week, with only older Handys and Sub Panamax vessels dipping slightly.

Handysize Alejandrina (1,700 TEU, Sep 2008, Hyundai Samho HI) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 17 mil, VV Value USD 17.7 mil.
