Weekly Vessel Valuations Report, November 25 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  25 November 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/weekly-vessel-valuations-report-november-25-2025

inWeekly Vessel Valuations Report25/11/2025

Bulkers:Larger dry bulk values firmed this week; an enbloc deal for two Newcastlemaxesand the sale of BW Japan both demonstrated strong pricing

2x Newcastlemax (208,000 DWT, 2020, New Times) sold in an enbloc deal including TC to Genco Shipping and Trading for USD 145.5 mil, VV Value USD 140.3 mil

Kamsarmax BW Japan (81,600 DWT, 2019, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold for USD 32.5 mil, VV Value USD 31.4 mil

Tankers:VV Tanker values have firmed this week especially for resale and modern vessels as S&P activity has increased

VLCC Cosgrand Lake (299,000 DWT, Feb 2006, Universal) sold SS/DD Due by COSCO Shipping Energy Transportation for USD 35 mil, VV Value 40.66 mil

Suezmax Stena Sunshine (159,000 DWT, Jan 2013, Samsung) sold to Unknown Greek buyers for USD 58 mil, VV Value 56.24 mil

MR2 (Chemical/Product) Amfitrion (50,100 DWT, Jan 2017, Samsung) sold by Capital Ship Management for USD 34.25 mil, VV Value 32.05 mil

Containers:Container values remain fairly stable this week, the only exception being older Panamax values continue to rise

Handy Container CNC Saturn (1,952 TEU, Nov 2020, Tsuneishi Cebu) sold by Nissen Kaiun for USD 36 mil, VV Value USD 37.1 mil

Handy Containers Contship Run & Contship Sea (1,574 TEU, 2007, Jiangsu Yangzijiang ) sold en bloc to MSC for USD 34 mil, VV Value USD 35.57 mil

Feedermax KR Celebes (728 TEU, Nov 2002, Dae Sun) sold by Gluck Shipping for USD 7 mil, VV Value USD 6.94 mil
