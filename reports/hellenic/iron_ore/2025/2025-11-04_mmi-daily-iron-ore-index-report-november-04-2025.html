MMI Daily Iron Ore Index Report November 04 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  04 November 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-november-04-2025

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices04/11/2025

Today, the price of iron ore futures continued to fluctuate weakly. The main contract I2601 finally closed at 775.5 yuan, down 1.71%from the previous working day. The mainstream spot price at ports fell by 2-8 yuan/ton from the previous working day. The transaction price of PB powder in Shandong area was 780-785 yuan/ton, down 2-5 yuan/ton from the previous day; the transaction price of PB powder in Hebei area was 786-792 yuan/ton, down 6-7 yuan/ton from the previous working day. The transaction atmosphere in the spot market is general, and the quotation of traders is generally active; steel mills purchase on demand and purchase cautiously. This week, the blast furnaces in Hebei Province gradually resumed production, and the amount of maintenance impact decreased by 72,600 tonnes compared with last week. However, the sintering production restriction measures still have a certain inhibitory effect on the demand for iron ore in the short term. In general, the short-term demand for iron ore may tend to be stable but the support is weak. In terms of news, the third round of coke price increase has been implemented, squeezing the profits of steel mills; and the consumption of steel has not increased significantly, the market has a strong expectation for the reduction of molten iron in the future, and the sentiment is weak. It is expected that the price of iron ore will maintain a weak trend in the short term.
