MMI Daily Iron Ore Index Report November 11 2025

Hellenic Shipping News  |  11 November 2025  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-november-11-2025

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices11/11/2025

DCE iron ore futures fluctuated rangebound today. The most-traded contract I2601 closed at 763 yuan/mt,up 0.2% from the previous trading day. Spot cargo prices at ports were basically flat compared to the previous trading day, with PB fines transaction prices in Shandong at 768–775 yuan/mt, basically unchanged from yesterday, and those in Hebei at 780–785 yuan/mt, flat from yesterday. Last week, SMM global iron ore shipments totaled 33.49 million mt, down about 3.9% WoW. According to SMM’s blast furnace maintenance survey, the hot metal output affected by BF maintenance this week was 1.1778 million mt, an increase of 11,900 mt WoW. The short-term daily average pig iron production saw relatively small changes. Spot iron ore prices remain strongly supported. Macro policy has temporarily entered a quiet period. In the short term, iron ore prices are expected to fluctuate rangebound.
