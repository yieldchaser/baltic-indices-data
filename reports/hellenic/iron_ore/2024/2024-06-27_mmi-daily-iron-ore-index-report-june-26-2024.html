MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report June 26 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  26 June 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-june-26-2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices27/06/2024

DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends. the main contract closed 826，an increase of 3.38% throughout the day;some traders were active to sell, and Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable . PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-820 yuan/mt; increased 5-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 825-835 yuan/mt; increased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM research, on June 26th, the blast furnace operating rate recorded by SMM was 94.50%, an increase of 0.59% compared to the previous month. The utilization rate of blast furnace production capacity was 96.33%, an increase of 0.55% compared to the previous period. The daily average iron production of the sample steel plant is 2.3025 million tons, an increase of 12000 tons compared to the previous month. And there is still room for growth next week. The increase in demand for iron ore provides strong support for ore prices. Meanwhile, there are market rumors that the country may introduce relevant measures to stabilize long-term investment, thereby enhancing market confidence. This series of factors together have led to a significant increase in mineral prices.
