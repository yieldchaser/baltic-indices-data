MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report December 17 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  17 December 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-december-17-2024

Today, DCE iron ore futures rose initially but then fell, with the most-traded I2505 contract closing at 796.5 yuan/mt, up0.06% for the day. Traders showed moderate enthusiasm for selling, while steel mills purchased as needed. Market transactions were moderate today. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 790 yuan/mt, stable compared to yesterday. In Tangshan, PB fines were traded at 800-805 yuan/mt, also stable compared to yesterday. In Handan, Hebei, most blast furnaces at steel mills affected by environmental protection-driven production restrictions have resumed operaƟons. However, as year-end approaches, annual maintenance plans have increased. According to SMM survey data on blast furnace maintenance, this week, the impact from maintenance on pig iron output was 1.8448 million mt, up 221,300 mt WoW. More blast furnace maintenance is planned for next week, with the estimated impact at 1.897 million mt, though the WoW increase is expected to narrow. The reduction in pig iron output has led to a decline in overall demand for iron ore, and iron ore prices are expected to remain stable with a weak trend in the short term.
