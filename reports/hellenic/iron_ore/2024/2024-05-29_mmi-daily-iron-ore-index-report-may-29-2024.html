inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices29/05/2024

DCE iron ore futures fall today, the main contract closed 891. an decrease of 1.11% throughout the day; Some tradersship according to market conditions; Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy. today’s overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 875-885 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 885-890 yuan/mt; decreased 0-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. This week, the pig iron production of SMM sample 190 steel mills slightly increased by 8800 tons. This week, three blast furnaces resumed production, and the planned maintenance of the blast furnaces was delayed, resulting in an increase in molten iron. In the future, as terminal demand enters the off-season, some steel mills have maintenance plans, and it is expected that the production of molten iron may shrink in June. However, considering the radiation of real estate policies to first tier cities and the relatively optimistic market sentiment, it is expected that mining prices will fluctuate at high levels in the short term.
