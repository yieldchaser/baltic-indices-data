MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report September 18 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  18 September 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-september-18-2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices18/09/2024

Today, iron ore futures dropped significantly, ﬂuctuating downward throughout the day. The most-traded contract I2501 finally closed at675 yuan/ mt, down 4.12% for the day. Market sentiment turned more cautious, and traders showed a lower willingness to sell; steel mills mainly made low- price purchases, with some starting to restock before the holiday. Today’s market trading atmosphere was sluggish. In Shandong, PB fines traded at around 675-680 yuan/mt, down 15-20 yuan/mt from before the holiday; in Tangshan, PB fines traded at around 695-700 yuan/mt, down 10-20 yuan/mt from before the holiday. During the holiday, the August social financing and real estate data were lower-than-expected, further dampening market sentiment, and iron ore prices returned to low levels. On the supply side, due to prices and domestic typhoon weather, the pressure on iron ore supply temporarily eased. Demand side, with the gradual resumption of blast furnaces in steel mills, pig iron production slightly increased. Additionally, with the National Day holiday approaching, some steel mills started to stock up in advance, improving the fundamentals of iron ore and providing some support to ore prices. Close attention should be paid to the US Fed’s interest rate decision tomorrow.
