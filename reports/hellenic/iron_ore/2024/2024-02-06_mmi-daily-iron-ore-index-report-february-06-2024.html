MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report February 06 2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices06/02/2024

DCE iron ore futures continued to ﬂuctuate in a day by 0.63%. As the Spring Festival approaches, coupled withweak market perfor- mance, the enthusiasm of traders to oﬀer is poor. However, steel mills mainly rely on observation and have not yet learned about market transactions. PBF at Shandong port quoted 980 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port quoted 990 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (February 3rd to February 9th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.7669 million tons, a decrease of 96600 tons compared to last week’s maintenance. The impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron next week (February 10th to February 16th) is 1.69 million tons, a decrease of 76900 tons compared to this week. There is still room for an increase in the production of molten iron in the future, while overseas transportation has declined, and there is not much contradiction in the fundamentals of iron ore. It is expected that iron ore prices will remain weak and stable before the Spring Festival.
