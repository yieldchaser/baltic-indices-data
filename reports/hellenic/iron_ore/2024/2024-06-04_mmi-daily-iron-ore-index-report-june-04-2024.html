Hellenic Shipping News  |  04 June 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-june-04-2024

DCE iron ore futures continued to fall in a day，the main contract closed 834， an decrease of 2.11% throughout theday; some traders were negative to sell; Some steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 830-840 yuan/mt; decreased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 855-860 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (June 1st to June 7th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.0117 million tons, a decrease of 63100 tons compared to last week’s maintenance. There is still a slight increase in the production of pig iron. However, in recent times, the macro positive sentiment has worsened, and the market’s expectations for off-season demand have deviated.
