MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report December 11 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  11 December 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-december-11-2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices11/12/2024

Today, the iron ore market underwent a contract rollover, with the most-traded contract switching to the I2505 contract.It remained stable in the morning but slightly declined in the afternoon. Throughout the day, it fluctuated downward, ultimately closing at 802, with a daily drop of 0.8%. Traders showed moderate willingness to sell, while steel mills purchased as needed. Today’s market transaction atmosphere was average. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 790-795 yuan/mt, down 5 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction prices for PB fines were 800-805 yuan/mt, also down 5 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to an SMM survey, on December 11, the operating rate of blast furnaces in 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 86.23%, down 2.71 percentage points MoM. The daily pig iron production of the sample steel mills was 2.3626 million mt, down 47,500 mt MoM. Recently, due to environmental protection-driven production restrictions in Hebei, many blast furnaces in steel mills have been shut down, leading to a significant decline in pig iron production. This has caused iron ore prices to fluctuate downward. Considering that important meetings are still being held recently, the downward space for iron ore is relatively limited.
