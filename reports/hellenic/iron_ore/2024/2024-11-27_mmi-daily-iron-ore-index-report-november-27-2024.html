MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report November 27 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  27 November 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-november-27-2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices27/11/2024

Today, iron ore ﬂuctuated upward, with the most-traded contract I2501 closing at 792 yuan, up 1.08% for the day. Traders were activelyselling, while steel mills remained cautious and had moderate purchase willingness. The market trading atmosphere was moderate. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 775-780 yuan/mt, up 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction prices for PB fines were 790-795 yuan/mt, also up 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to an SMM survey, on November 27, the operating rate of blast furnaces in 242 surveyed steel mills was 89.16%, down 0.67% WoW. The capacity utilisation rate of blast furnaces was 89.75%, down 0.80% WoW. The daily pig iron production of the sample steel mills was 2.4 million mt, down 21,500 mt WoW. The fundamentals of iron ore continued to weaken. However, market rumors suggested that the Politburo meeting in December might be held earlier, which boosted market expectations. Additionally, some steel mills started purchasing imported cargo to increase inventory, providing strong support for ore prices.
