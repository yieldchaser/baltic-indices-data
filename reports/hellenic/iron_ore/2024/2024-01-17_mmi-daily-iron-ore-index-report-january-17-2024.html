MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report January 17 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  17 January 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-january-17-2024

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices17/01/2024

The iron ore future price continued to shake down today, the main contract I2405 closed at 925, down 1.33%throughout the day. Iron ore spot price in the morning rose slightly 2-3 yuan/mt or so, but the inquiry was relatively less. Some steel mills continued the rhythm of just needing to purchase, the actual transaction is limited throughout the day, and the trading sentiment was weak. PBF in the Shandong region quoted at 1005 yuan/mt in the morning, which was changed limited from yesterday; PBF at Tangshan early morning offered at 1015-1020 yuan/mt or so, up 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to SMM research, the blast furnace operating rate of domestic steel mills is 88.11%, which is unchanged from the last week. The utilization rate of blast furnace capacity was 89.11%, up 0.3% from the previous period. The average daily output of pig iron at the sample steel mill was 2.13Mt, an increase of 0.72Mt from the previous month. Although this week’s blast furnace maintenance impact has declined, the macro sentiment is oriented, the overall spot market transaction is weak, and iron ore prices are expected to continue weak shocks in the short term.
