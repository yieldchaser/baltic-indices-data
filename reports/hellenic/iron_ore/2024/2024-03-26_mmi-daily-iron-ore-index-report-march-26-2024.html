DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends，the main contract closed 814.5，an decrease of 3.72% throughout the day;Traders are mostly trading cautiously in response to market conditions, while steel mills are showing reduced inquiries and continue to focus on purchasing based on demand. Market transactions are relatively sluggish. In Shandong, the transaction price of PB powder is in the range of 805-810 yuan/ton, a decrease of around 20 yuan compared to yesterday.According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron production this week (March 23 to March 29) is 188.99 million tons, a decrease of 6.78 million tons compared to last week. The estimated impact of blast furnace maintenance next week (March 30 to April 5) is 175.61 million tons, a decrease.
