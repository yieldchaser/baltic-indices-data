MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report September 19 2024

Hellenic Shipping News  |  19 September 2024  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-september-19-2024

Today, iron ore rebounded from its bottom and ﬂuctuated upward throughout the day. The most-traded contract I2501 finally closedat 693 yuan/ mt, with a daily increase of 1.69%. Speculative trading among traders increased; steel mills mainly remained on the sidelines, with some planning tenders. Today’s market transaction atmosphere was average. The transaction price of PB fines in Shandong was around 675-695 yuan/mt, up 0-20 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction price of PB fines was around 695-715 yuan/mt, up 0-15 yuan/mt from yesterday. In the early morning, the FOMC meeting announced that the US Fed would cut interest rates by 50 basis points, slightly exceeding market expectations. During the period from the announcement to Powell’s speech, the overseas market generally reacted with a “buy the rumor, sell the news” sentiment, causing iron ore futures to slightly retreat after opening. However, in the short term, the rate cut is beneficial to the real estate and manufacturing sectors. This also led to increased market expectations for macroeconomic policies in the fourth quarter, driving the futures market to rise rapidly. Driven by macro factors and pre-holiday restocking, it is expected that iron ore prices will continue to ﬂuctuate upward in the short term.
