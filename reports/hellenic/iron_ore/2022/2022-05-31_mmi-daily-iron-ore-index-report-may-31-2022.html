MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report May 31 2022

Hellenic Shipping News  |  31 May 2022  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-may-31-2022

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices31/05/2022

May 31st, 2022 CFR Qingdao, USD/dry tonne DCE iron ore futures opened shock run, the main contract 2209 closed at 892.5, up 2.06%throughout the day. Traders are enthusiastic about shipping, steel mills are on the sidelines, and the market transaction atmosphere is cold. The transaction price of PBF in Shandong is concentrated in 975-990 yuan /mt, up 5-15 yuan /mt from yesterday’s price, and the transaction price of super special powder in Shandong is 790 yuan / mt, increase 18 yuan /mt from yesterday’s price. The transaction price of PB powder in Tangshan area is 975-980 yuan / mt, increase of 10-15 yuan / mt from yesterday’s price. According to SMM tracking data, a total of 81 ships arrived at China’s major ports from 5.23 to 5.29 days, and the arrival volume is expected to be 12.52 million tons, and the arrival volume is 390,000 tons lower than the previous period, an increase of 48,000 tons over the same period last year. During the period, Australian shipments increased by 1.04 million tonnes to 19.83 million tonnes, an increase of 4.27 million tonnes from the same period last year, while Brazilian shipments shipments decreased by 890,000 tonnes to 6.04 million tonnes in the current period, a decrease of 280,000 tonnes from the same period last year. Imported mine arrivals in the current period decreased slightly sequentially, while shipments to Australia and Brazil increased slightly. Shipments to Australia continue to increase significantly. However, Shipments from Brazil have declined, and there is still room for iron ore shipments overseas. At present, the demand for iron ore is stable and increasing, and steel mills have not reduced production due to losses and high inventories, and ore prices have strong support. However, considering the inflated or policy intervention of ore prices, it is expected that mineral prices will continue to operate in a wide range in the short term.
