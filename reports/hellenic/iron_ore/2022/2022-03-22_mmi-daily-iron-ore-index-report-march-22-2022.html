MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report March 22 2022

Hellenic Shipping News  |  22 March 2022  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-march-22-2022

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices22/03/2022

DCE iron ore futures market falling trends，an decrease of 3.62% throughout the day; the main contract closed 799. The spot quotation inthe morning almost kept steady. but as the disk continues to decline, some traders decreased the price. steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 940-945 yuan/ mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 640-645 yuan/mt; decreased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Tianjin port dealt 673 yuan/mt; decreased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. SMM tracking data shows that a total of 70 ships arrived at major ports in China from March 14 to March 20, and the arrival cargo volume is estimated to be 10.753 million tons, which is 470,000 tons more than the previous period and 2.467 million tons less than the same period last year. During this period, The outbound shipments from Australia increased by 1.14 million tons to 17.25 million tons, an increase of 3.01 million tons over the same period last year. Brazil’s outbound shipments increased by 0.8 million tons to 4.67 million tons, down 1.28 million tons compared with the same period last year. The amount of imported ore to Hong Kong increased slightly in this period, while the overall shipment from Australia and Brazil increased significantly. Recently, the domestic epidemic has intensified, especially in Shandong and Tangshan, two major steel producing areas, production and transportation have been affected, which will curb the demand for iron ore in the short term, and the price of ore pressure shock operation.
