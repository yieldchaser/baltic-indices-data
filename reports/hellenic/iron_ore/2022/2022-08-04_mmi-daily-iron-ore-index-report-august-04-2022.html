MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report August 04 2022

Hellenic Shipping News  |  04 August 2022  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-august-04-2022

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices04/08/2022

DCE iron ore was was shocked and keep downward all the day.The main contract closed at 756.5,decreased of 4.78%. Traders are generallymotivated to ship, Speculative demand increases. Steel mills are mainly on the sidelines, and market turnover continues to pick up.PBF at Shandong port dealt 755-778 yuan/mt;,decrease 25 yuan/mt than yesterday;SSF at Shandong port dealt 630 yuan/mt,decrease 30yuan/mt.PBF at Tangshan dealt 760 yuan/mt,decrease 10-30 yuan/mt than yesterday. From a fundamental point of view, at present, some steel plants in production are temporarily not in full production, and the procurement of iron ore is mainly based on rigid demand. The demand of Hebei and Shanxi regions is affected by the requirements of environmental protection and production restriction, and the resumption progress of steel mills is affected to a certain extent. Steel mills are also cautious in purchasing, and it is expected that the increment of molten iron will be relatively slow, putting pressure on the upward price of ore. In the recent two periods, the port dredging volume of iron ore is low, and there is a certain pressure on the port inventory. At present, it may take the initiative to go to the warehouse.
