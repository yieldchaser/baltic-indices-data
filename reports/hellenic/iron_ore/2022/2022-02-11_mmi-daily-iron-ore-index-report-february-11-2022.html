MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report February 11 2022

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices11/02/2022

DCE iron ore futures continued to fluctuate in a day by 0.31%.the main contract closed 805. the quotation in the morning was increased by20-30 yuan/mt over yesterday. some traders were active to sell, but some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see,and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today’s overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 970-980 yuan/mt, increased 0-15 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 630-635 yuan/mt, increased 10-25 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of Feb. 11, stocks at 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 154.19 million tons, up 5.17 million tons from before the Spring Festival. And the average daily port clearance volume significantly decreased from 722,000 tons to 2.365 million tons. On the one hand, during the Spring Festiva, the efficiency of major domestic ports decreases; In addition, environmental protection control in some areas has been tightened recently, and some ports have been required to stop using diesel trucks ,and gas trucks with national V and below emission standards have been evacuated from the ports. The efficiency of the evacuation continues to decline, leading to the obvious accumulation of port inventory. Considering the impact of environmental protection restrictions such as winter Olympics and heating season this week, it is difficult to see a significant increase in daily port opening, and port inventory is expected to maintain a relatively high level. Ore prices are under pressure. In addition, the National Development and Reform Commission will recently send a joint research team to some commodity exchanges, key ports to carry out iron ore market supervision research, iron ore prices are expected to continue to shock next week.
