inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices09/02/2022

DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends, an decrease of 5.90% throughout the day; the main contract closed 781. the quotationin the morning was decreased by 30 yuan/mt over yesterday. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 927-930 yuan/mt, decreased 35-40 yuan/mt over yesterday; SSF at Shandong port dealt 590-595 yuan/mt, decreased 40-45 yuan/mt over yesterday; PBF at Tangshan port dealt925-930 yuan/mt, decreased 40-45 yuan/mt over yesterday; Yesterday, the northern region ushered in a new wave of heavy pollution weather, hebei, Henan, Shandong, Shanxi and other places to further tighten environmental protection limits on production; Superimposed steel mills are currently mainly to consume inventory, iron ore demand is weak. On the other hand, the price of iron ore has been rising recently. The National Development and Reform Commission issued a statement that it is highly concerned about the change of iron ore price and will further study and take effective measures to ensure the smooth operation of iron ore market price. Ore prices began to enter the shock callback stage. Iron ore is expected to continue to weak shock tomorrow.
