inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices16/09/2022

DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.24% today, the main contract closed 715. Some traders were active to sell. More steel mills tended topurchase, and purchasing enthusiasm is high. Total transactions returned actively.PBF at Shandong port dealt 750-757 yuan/mt; decreased 5-15 yuan/mt than yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 748-767 yuan/mt;decreased 10-20yuan/mt than yesterday. As of September 16, the inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM totaled 133.24 million tons, a slight cumulative 360000 tons compared with last week, and an increase of 2.46 million tons compared with the same period last year. The average daily port dredging volume of imported ore in this period decreased by 187000 tons to 2712000 tons on a weekly basis. After the Mid Autumn Festival, the power of steel mills to replenish warehouses has weakened, resulting in a decline in the volume of port dredging. At present, the demand side is affected by the policy before the “Twentieth National Congress” and the finished product side, and the ore price fluctuates slightly.
