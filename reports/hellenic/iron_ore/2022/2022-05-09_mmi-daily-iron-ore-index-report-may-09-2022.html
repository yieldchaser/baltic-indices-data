inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices09/05/2022

DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends by 5.78%. the main contract closed 806.5. the quotation in the morning was decreased by10-20 yuan/mt from last week. some traders were negative to sell, steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 920-925 yuan/mt; decreased 20-25 yuan/mt from Last week. SSF at Shandong port dealt 710 yuan/mt; decreased 20 yuan/mt from Last week. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 920 yuan/mt; decreased 25 yuan/mt from Last week. SSF at Shandong port dealt 715 yuan/mt; decreased 15 yuan/ mt from Last week. Recently, affected by the international macro monetary environment and downstream demand is not as expected and other factors, the market mentality is weak, futures plate has a callback. Port spot prices followed. However, SMM research in Shandong, Shanxi, Hebei, Jiangsu and other places of steel production is relatively stable, under the influence of capital and profit losses, iron ore inventory running at a low level, just need to replenish the stock will be strong, give ore price support. Iron ore prices are expected to shock broadly in the short term.
