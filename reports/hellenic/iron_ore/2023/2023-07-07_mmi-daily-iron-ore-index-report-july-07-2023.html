MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report July 07 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  07 July 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-july-07-2023

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices07/07/2023

DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.87% today, the main contract closed at 812.5.The traders’ willingness to ship isless.The steel mills are not active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is worse. PBF at Tangshan port deal 903 yuan/mt. In terms of overseas news, the probability of the Federal Reserve raising interest rates in July has soared to more than 95%, risky assets have plunged across the board, the U.S. Dollar Index has reversed in a V-shape, and the RMB exchange rate has fluctuated sharply. In terms of domestic news, the core judgment of the National Economic Forum is that there is no large-scale stimulus policy, and the overall market sentiment is relatively pessimistic. Fundamentals As of today, the 35 ports tracked by SMM have a total inventory of 1.2256 million tons, a decrease of 1 million tons compared to last week. The daily dredging volume of imported ore has increased by 45000 tons to 3.032 million tons compared to last week. With the support of high-speed rail, iron ore prices are unlikely to fall deeply, but the news has weakened, and market confidence may be shaken. In the short term, iron ore prices are mainly volatile.
