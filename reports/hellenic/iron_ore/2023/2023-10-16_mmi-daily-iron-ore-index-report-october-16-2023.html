inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices16/10/2023

DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends by 2.86%. the main contract I2401 closed 862,some traders were active to sell, but steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high but steel mills purchased on demand few transactions is poor. PBF at Shandong port dealt 930-935 yuan/mt; increased 8-10 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Shandong port dealt 953 yuan/mt; increased 10 yuan/mt from last Friday. The total global iron ore shipment volume of SMM is 33.07 million tons, an increase of 10% compared to the previous month. Among them, shipments from Australia and Brazil have both shown significant growth, but the arrival volume has slightly decreased due to the impact of domestic typhoons. In the short term, the tight inventory situation at the port is difficult to alleviate, providing strong support for mining prices. In addition, the purchase restriction policy in the Hangzhou real estate market was introduced today, exceeding market expectations and boosting market confidence. In the future, the short-term impact of negative feedback is relatively small, which is not enough to suppress ore prices. The addition of port inventory provides support for spot goods, and high premiums also drive up iron ore prices. However, considering the loss situation of steel mills and weak willingness to purchase iron ore, the short-term dilemma of ore prices may continue to fluctuate.
