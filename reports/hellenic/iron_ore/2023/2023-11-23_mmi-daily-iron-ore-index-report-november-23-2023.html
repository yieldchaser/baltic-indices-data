MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report November 23 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  23 November 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-november-23-2023

DCE iron ore futures fall today, The main contract I2401 plummeted significantly in theafternoon and ultimately closed at 976.5, a decrease of 0.86% throughout the day. some traders were negative to sell, Some traders are temporarily unable to ship due to losses. The purchasing enthusiasm of steel mills has increased, with low price procurement being the main focus. The market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 995 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1000-1015 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. This morning, the National Development and Reform Commission strengthened the supervision of iron ore futures and spot linkage. Iron ore fell in the market. From the industrial perspective, the demand for building materials has slightly decreased month on month this week, while the demand for hot rolls has increased in a virtual month on month manner. Overall, the apparent demand for finished products has rebounded and inventory continues to decline, indicating strong resilience in terminal demand. Considering that macro expectations are still strong, it is expected that there will be limited downward space for short-term ore prices once again. Continue oscillating operation.
