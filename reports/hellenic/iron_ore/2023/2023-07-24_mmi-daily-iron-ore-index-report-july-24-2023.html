The total global iron ore shipment volume of SMM is 29.65 million tons, a decrease of 1.8% compared to the previous month. The arrival volume has slightly increased. On the demand side, aﬀected by Tangshan’s environmental protection production restrictions, the number of blast furnace shutdowns in steel mills continues to increase. The supply increase and demand decrease have weakened the support for ore prices. However, as the end of the month approaches, market expectations for important meetings remain strong, and market sentiment remains strong.
