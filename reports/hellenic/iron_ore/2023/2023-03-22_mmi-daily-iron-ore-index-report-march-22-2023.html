From the perspective of the news, overseas economic events continue to affect domestic market sentiment, and the iron ore futures market conƟnues to weaken. Fundamentally, according to SMM statistics, the blast furnace operating rate was 93.46%, up 0.3% from the previous period; The utilization rate of blast furnace capacity increased by 0.35% to 94.23% on a month-on-month basis, and the current amount of molten iron in the blast furnace provides some support to the ore price. Pessimistic news such as pressure cuts, production restrictions, regulation, and external market declines have put pressure on ore prices, which are expected to be dominated by range fluctuations in the short term.
