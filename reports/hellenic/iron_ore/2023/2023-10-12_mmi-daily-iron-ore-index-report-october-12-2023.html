MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report October 12 2023

inChinese iron ore and steelmaking prices12/10/2023

DCE iron ore futures rose by 1.64% today, the main contract closed at 837. The steel mills are active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 919-920 yuan/mt. PBF at Tangshan port deal 935-957 yuan/mt. From the news, the Ministry of Housing and Urban Rural Development stated that mega cities are actively and steadily promoting the transformation of urban villages, and the policy environment is warming up, boosting market sentiment. In terms of the industrial chain, the demand for inventory reduction of finished products is acceptable, and the negative feedback risk is reduced. According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, all major varieties of inventory decreased this Thursday, with steel mills experiencing a decline in profits and weaker production enthusiasm. Speculative demand has decreased, and there are still some cases of just in need of goods. As the heating season approaches in the northern region, the production of sintering machines may be further tightened, and the demand for blocks and balls is still relatively optimistic. However, considering the strong price of coke, the cost-effectiveness of lump ore may be lacking. At present, the supply of iron ore is loose and the demand is declining. In the future, the focus will be on industrial policies and steel mill production. In the short term, it is recommended to carry out a small amount of short selling operations, while the spot market is mainly cautious and wait-and-see.
