MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report December 04 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  04 December 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-december-04-2023

DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.14 % today, the main contract closed at 958. The steel mills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Tangshan port deal 985 yuan/mt. From the news perspective, the recent Politburo meeting and economic meeting will give the market some confidence in the information released by the meeting. Iron ore is in a tight equilibrium stage: the overseas shipping volume on the supply side is at a high level, with an increase compared to the previous month, and the arrival volume at major ports in China has slightly increased by 3%; On the demand side, due to the steel mill’s maintenance plan, the average production of molten iron fluctuated slightly this Sunday, but the magnitude was not significant; The accumulated inventory at the port has expanded, but the absolute value is relatively low. From the perspective of the industrial chain, the demand for finished products is gradually decreasing, and the profits of steel mills are being compressed due to the increase in coke prices and the high absolute prices of iron ore. However, the profits of the steel mill have not been reduced to cash flow, and the pace of replenishing raw materials may slow down, but the current impact is not significant. In the short term, it is expected that the price of iron ore will fluctuate widely.
