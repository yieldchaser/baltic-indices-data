MMi Daily Iron Ore Index Report May 10 2023

Hellenic Shipping News  |  10 May 2023  |  https://www.hellenicshippingnews.com/mmi-daily-iron-ore-index-report-may-10-2023

DCE iron ore was choppy in the day, the main contract I2309 closed 724,an increase of 0.28% throughout the day. Traders ship according tomarket conditions. and steel mills purchase with caution. PBF at Shandong port dealt 788-805 yuan/mt; prices are mixed today over yesterday. SSF at Tangshan port dealt 663 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. DCE iron ore opened up and then fell, with significant fluctuations due to the impact of news. The overall fundamentals of iron ore have not changed much, but the transaction of building materials has been poor, which has weakened the market mentality. In addition, in the early stage, iron ore prices fell excessively, and the current market valuation is low, with a large basis difference for the 2309 period. The market is bullish and optimistic, and it is expected that short-term ore prices will continue to fluctuate.
