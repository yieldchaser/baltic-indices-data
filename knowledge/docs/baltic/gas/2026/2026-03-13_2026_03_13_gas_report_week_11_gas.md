## Summary
LNG
The LNG spot market cooled this week, with rates correcting across all major routes as the recent rally began to unwind. A growing tonnage list and fewer cargoes weighed on sentiment, driving a steady decline in earnings. On the BLNG1 Australia-Japan route, 174k cbm vessels fell $33,600 week-on-week to settle at $158,000/day, as the Pacific market began to ease from the previous week's highs.
