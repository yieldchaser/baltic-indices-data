## Summary
LNG
The LNG spot market continued to cool this week as the short-term spike in sentiment driven by tensions in the Middle East began to weaken. As the week progressed, a growing tonnage list and easing urgency from charterers pushed rates lower across both basins. On the BLNG1 Australia-Japan route, 174k cbm vessels declined $29,900 week-on-week to settle at $104,900/day, reflecting the broader softening in the Pacific market.
