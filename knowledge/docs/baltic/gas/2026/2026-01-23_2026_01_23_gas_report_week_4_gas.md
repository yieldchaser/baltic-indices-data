## Summary
LNG
The LNG spot market remained under pressure this week, with rates continuing to decline across all major routes. A lengthy tonnage list and limited fresh cargo activity continued to weigh on sentiment, particularly in the Atlantic, allowing charterers to push rates lower. On the BLNG1 Australia-Japan route, 174,000 cbm vessel earnings fell by $4,300 to $34,600/day.
