## Summary
LNG
The LNG spot market saw a strong week, with the Atlantic firming and the Pacific remaining flat. With a few uncovered cargoes in the West and a tightening tonnage list at the front end of the fixing window, it led to an upward correction in rates. On the BLNG1 Australia-Japan route, 174k cbm vessels slipped $400 to $27,400/day.
