## Summary
LNG
The LNG spot market was broadly steady but subdued this week. With much of the market attending events in Doha and preparations for IE Week underway, activity was notably thin, resulting in a particularly quiet market. On the BLNG1 Australia-Japan route, 174k cbm vessels slipped $1,200 to $29,400/day, with the East continuing to show greater resilience than the West.
