## Summary
Main
With the pause in US tariffs over for now, we have seen a further gradual decline in freight costs per box on the FBX routes to the US. This will be partly due to savvy shippers, moving all their imports out of China into the US whilst the tariffs were on hold for 90 days, so we may have seen an early peak season. Interestingly trade to the Continent has grown in volume and freight costs, and look to be seeing a shift in trade dynamics as a side effect of the US tariffs, which will be worth watching in the coming months.
