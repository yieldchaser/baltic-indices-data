## Summary
Container
2026 is looking to be an interesting year for the container shipping market. With a massive orderbook, limited scrapping and Red Sea transits looking to resume, tonne miles will surely drop and overcapacity of tonnage will become an issue. This will cause liner companies to struggle to find ways of maintaining freight rates, resulting in them blanking services or scrapping older vessels.
