Capesize
The market endured a broadly softer week, with sentiment cooling across both the Pacific and Atlantic as the BCI 182 5TC slid from the low $31,000s to finish closer to $26,468. C5 corrected sharply from the mid $9.00s down into the low-to-mid $8.00s.
