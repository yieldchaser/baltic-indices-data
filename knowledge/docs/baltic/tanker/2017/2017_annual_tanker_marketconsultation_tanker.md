This will be the main opportunity of the year for members to discuss the routes and vessels used within the current suite of Baltic tanker market benchmarks as well as upcoming changes and proposals. The consultation takes place before the Baltic's Freight and Commodities Forum at the same venue.

## Main
This will be the main opportunity of the year for members to discuss the routes and vessels used within the current suite of Baltic tanker market benchmarks as well as upcoming changes and proposals.
The consultation takes place before the Baltic's Freight and Commodities Forum at the same venue.
