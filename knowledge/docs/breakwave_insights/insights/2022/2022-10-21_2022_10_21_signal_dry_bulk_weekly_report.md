## Summary
Main
Freight rates in the Capesize and Panamax segments, as well as in the smaller vessel categories, weakened in the third week of October, indicating slower percentage demand growth. However, dry bulk coal flows continue to see an increase in shipments to Europe, with the Netherlands, Germany, Italy, and Spain as the main destinations, and Russia still considered the main country of origin. ## Main
