## Main
in Weekly Shipbuilding Reports 08/03/2026
Image reference: 2026-03-08_hd-hyundai-samho-snags-179-million-oil-tanker-order-in-greec_img1.jpg
HD Hyundai Samho said Friday it has secured a 263.2 billion won ($179 milion) order to build two oil tankers for a shipping company in Greece.
The deal covers two 157,000-deadweight-ton tankers, with deliveries scheduled by May 2029, the company said in a regulatory filing. The name of the Greece-based client was not disclosed.
HD Hyundai Samho is one of three shipbuilding affiliates under HD Korea Shipbuilding & Offshore Engineering (HD KSOE), along with HD Hyundai Heavy Industries and HD Hyundai Mipo Dockyard.
HD KSOE is a sub-holding company of HD Hyundai, Korea's leading shipbuilding group.
HD KSOE aims to secure $23.31 billion worth of orders this year, up from $18.16 billion last year. Source: Korea JoongAng Daily
