## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 16/08/2022
Image reference: 2022-08-16_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-august-16-2022_img1.png
Bulkers: Bulker values have softened, driven by a softening in earnings.
Diana Shipping has acquired 9 Ultramax Vessels (between 60,300 & 60,500 DWT, Aug 2015 to November 2018, Mitsui Tamano & Japan Marine United) for a total of USD 330 mil (comprised of USD 220 mil cash and USD 110 mil shares), VV en bloc value USD 282 mil.
Supramax Zhong Liang Dong Nan (52,600 DWT, Apr 2001, Shin Kurushima Onishi) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.59 mil, VV Value USD 11.46 mil - SS Due.
Tankers: Suezmax values have firmed.
Suezmax Ridgebury Mary Selena (146,500 DWT, Sep 2006, Universal) sold to Avin International for USD 31.00 mil, VV Value USD 28.32 mil.
MR2 Targale (52,600 DWT, Mar 2007, 3 Maj Brodogradiliste) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.20 mil, VV Value USD 16.92 mil - (more).
MRs Ridgebury Alexandra Z, Ridgebury Cindy A, Ridgebury Rosemary E (50,300 DWT, May 2009, SPP) sold to Middle Eastern buyers in an en bloc deal for USD 66.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 59.85 mil.
Containers: Container values have softened small amounts, driven by earnings.
