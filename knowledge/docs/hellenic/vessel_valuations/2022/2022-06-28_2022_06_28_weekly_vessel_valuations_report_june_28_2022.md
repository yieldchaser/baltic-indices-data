Aframax Maran Sagitta (105,100 DWT, Sep 2009, Hyundai Heavy Ind) sold to Performance Shipping Inc for USD 27.58 mil, VV Value USD 25.12 mil - DD Due, BWTS Fitted.
LR1 Energy Centurion (75,000 DWT, Aug 2008, Sungdong) sold to unknown Greek buyers for USD 18.00 mil, VV Value USD 16.80 mil.
MR2 Energy Panther (46,600 DWT, Nov 2008, Sungdong) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16.00 mil, VV Value USD 16.66 mil.
MR1 (Chem / Product) Baltic Favour (37,100 DWT, Feb 2006, Hyundai Mipo) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.10 mil, VV Value USD 11.18 mil - BWTS Fitted.
Small Clean Tanker (Chem/Prod) Maersk Borneo (29,000 DWT, Apr 2007, CSSC OME) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.80 mil, VV Value USD 10.87 mil - SS Passed.
