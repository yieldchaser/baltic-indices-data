## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 25/01/2022
Image reference: 2022-01-25_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-january-25-2022_img1.png
Tanker: Tanker values have softened.
VLCC Sea Lynx (318,300 DWT, Nov 2004, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold to Middle Eastern buyers for USD 32.20 mil, VV value USD 29.41 mil - BWTS Fitted.
Suezmax Nordic Mistral (164,200 DWT, Oct 2002, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold for USD 15.80 mil, VV value USD 17.29 mil.
Suezmax Kaveri Spirit (159,100 DWT, Jan 2004, Hyundai Heavy Ind) sold for USD 15.80 mil, VV value USD 17.98 mil - DD Due.
MR1 Hafnia Karava (40,000 DWT, Mar 2007, Saiki) sold for USD 9.00 mil, VV value USD 10.81 mil.
Small Clean Tankers LS Anne and LS Jamie (5,800 DWT, Aug 2008 & Mar 2009, Soli) sold in an en bloc deal for USD 9.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 10.48 mil - DD Due.
Bulker: Older tonnage has softened.
Panamax Energy Triton (82,100 DWT, May 2012, Tsuneishi Zosen) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 25.00 mil, VV value USD 25.03 mil - BWTS fitted.
Supramax HTC Delta (56,500 DWT, Jun 2014, Taizhou Sanfu) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 18.00 mil, VV value USD 18.16 mil - DD Due, BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker Xing Yuan Hai (34,400 DWT, Dec 2015, Namura) sold to European buyers for USD 23.75 mil, VV value USD 23.88 mil - Inc Charter, BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker Intrepid Eagle (33,500 DWT, Apr 2013, Samjin Shipbuilding) sold for USD 15.60 mil, VV value USD 15.63 mil - Inc Charter.
Handy Bulker Teda (32,400 DWT, Sept 2006, Kanda) sold to Turkish buyers for USD 13.80 mil, VV value USD 14.26 mil - SS/DD Passed, BWTS fitted.
Container: Container values have firmed.
Post Panamax Catherine C & Leo C (6,178 TEU, Sep 2001 - Jan 2002, Samsung) sold in an en bloc deal for USD 130.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 126.86 mil - SS/DD Due, Including Charter.
Sub Panamax AS Patricia (2,474 TEU, Sep 2006, SSW Fahr) sold for USD 34.30 mil, VV value 32.44 mil - SS Passed.
