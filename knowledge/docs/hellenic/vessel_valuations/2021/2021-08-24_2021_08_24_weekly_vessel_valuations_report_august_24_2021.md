## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 24/08/2021
Image reference: 2021-08-24_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-august-24-2021_img1.png
Tanker: Tanker values have softened.
Small Chemical Tanker Bochem Antwerp (19,800 DWT, Nov 2011, Kitanihon Zosen) sold to Vietnamese buyers for USD 15.60 mil, VV value USD 15.24 mil - SS/DD Due.

Supramax Ashiya Star (52,200 DWT, Oct 2006, Oshima) sold for USD 13.80 mil, VV value USD 13.32 mil - SS Passed, BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker Pacific Bulker (36,300 DWT, Mar 2015, Shikoku Dockyard) sold to Hong Kong buyers for USD 21.00 mil, VV value USD 20.22 mil - BWTS fitted.
Container: Container values have firmed.
No sales to report.
