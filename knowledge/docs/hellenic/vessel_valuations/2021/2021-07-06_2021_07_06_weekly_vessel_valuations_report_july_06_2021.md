## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 06/07/2021
Image reference: 2021-07-06_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-july-06-2021_img1.png
Tanker: Mid age Aframax values have softened.
VLCCs Nissos Antiparos and Nissos Santorini (319,000 DWT, Jul 2019, Hyundai Heavy Ind) sold to Frontline Ltd in an en bloc deal for USD 180.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 173.96 mil.
VLCC Maran Triton (305,000 DWT, Jul 2003, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold to Chinese buyers for USD 29.00 mil, VV value USD 29.12 mil.
VLCCs Hull 3211 and Hull 3212 (300,000 DWT, Mar & May 2022, Hyundai Heavy Ind) sold to Okeanis Eco Tankers in an en bloc deal for USD 194.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 186.18 mil - Internal sale.
MR2 Navig8 Tanzanite (49,000 DWT, Nov 2016, STX Offshore) sold to Navig8 Chemical Tankers for USD 30.70 mil, VV value USD 30.14 mil - Purchase Option.
MR2 Jasmine Express (47,000 DWT, Jun 2004, Onomichi Dockyard) sold to Greek buyers for USD 7.60 mil, VV value USD 8.31 mil.
Bulker: Bulker values have firmed slightly.
Capesizes Navios Bonavis (180,000 DWT, Jun 2009, Daewoo), Navios Ray (179,500 DWT, Mar 2012, Hanjin Subic) and Navios Azimuth (179,200 DWT, Feb 2011, Sungdong) sold to Navios Maritime Partners LP in an en bloc deal for USD 88.00 mil, VV en bloc value USD 87.26 mil - Internal sale.
Capesize Springbank (178,100 DWT, Nov 2010, New Times) sold to Kassian Maritime for USD 26.50 mil, VV value 26.53 mil - BWTS fitted.
Panamax Koulitsa (76,900 DWT, Apr 2003, Oshima) sold to UAE buyers for USD 13.60 mil, VV value 11.83 mil - DD Passed, BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker N Discovery (37,500 DWT, Oct 2012, Zhejiang Ouhua) sold to Costamare for USD 14.20 mil, VV value 15.58 mil.
Handy Bulker Interlink Parity (37,300 DWT, Mar 2012, Huatai HI) sold to Costamare for USD 14.75 mil, VV value 14.04 mil.
Handy Bulker Dream Island (34,500 DWT, Aug 2015, Hakodate Dock) sold to Greek buyers for USD 18.90 mil, VV value 18.85 mil - BWTS fitted.
Handy Bulker King Cotton (33,600 DWT, Oct 2011, Shin Kurushima) sold for USD 14.00 mil, VV value 15.14 mil - SS/DD Due.
Container: Handy and Feedermax values have firmed.
No sales with disclosed prices have been confirmed this week.
