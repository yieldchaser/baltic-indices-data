Tankers: Large Tanker values continued to firm. The acquisition of Suezmax Silverway (157,800 DWT, Jan 2017, Sungdong) by MSC appears to signal growing appetite for the sector, with rumours emerging that the Owner is actively seeking additional Suezmax vessels, pointing to the early stages of a potential buying spree

Containers: Older Sub Panamax containers are up a little this week following a recent en bloc sale to MSC, older Feedermaxes are down similarly
Sub Panamax Containers Margarete Schulte and Lucie Schulte (2,602 TEU, 2006, STX Offshore) sold to MSC for USD 50 mil en bloc, VV Value USD 49 mil.
Feedermax Erasmus Oasis (1,049 TEU, Jun 2008, Dae Sun) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 11 mil, VV Value USD 12.9 mil
