## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 21/10/2025
Image reference: 2025-10-21_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-october-21-2025_img1.png
Bulkers: VV Bulker values have mostly remained stable this week, with older Handysize vesselsseeing an increase due to sales such as Bright Hope and TBC Passion
Capesize BC Hebei No 1 (182,400 DWT, Oct 2009, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 25 mil, VV Value USD 26 mil
Panamax BC Porto Limnioni (73,500 DWT, Jun 2006, Jiangnan Shanghai Changxing HI) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 8.5 mil, VV Value USD 8.61 mil
Supramax BC Icarius (55,900 DWT, Apr 2007, Mitsui Tamano) sold to unknown Chinese buyers for USD 11.75 mil, VV Value USD 12.02 mil
Handy BC Bright Hope (28,200 DWT, Sep 2010, Imabari) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 9.8 mil, VV Value USD 9.82 mil
Handy BC TBC Passion (38,200 DWT, Jul 2011, Imabari) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 14.4 mil, VV Value USD 13.85 mil
Tankers: S&P activity has picked up this week with the majority of VV Tanker values firming as a result.
VLCC Landbridge Prosperity & Landbridge Fortune (308,300 DWT, 2016, Dalian Shipbuilding Industry Corp) sold en bloc to Trafigura Beheer BV for USD 170 mil, VV Value USD 173.3 mil
Suezmax Konya (163,800 DWT, May 2005, Hyundai Heavy Ind Ulsan Shipyard) sold SS/DD Passed to undisclosed buyers for USD 28.4 mil, VV Value USD 27.18 mil
Suezmax Eco Beverly Hills & Eco Bel Air (157,000 DWT, 2019, Hyundai Samho Heavy Ind) sold en bloc to Performance Shipping for USD 150.88 mil, VV Value USD 151.30 mil
Aframax Minerva Lisa & Minerva Iris (103,800 DWT, 2004, Samsung) sold en bloc to Unknown UAE buyers for USD 43.5 mil, VV Value USD 43.73 mil
MR2 (Chemical/Product) Stavanger Poseidon (50,000 DWT, Aug 2020, Hyundai Vietnam Shipbuilding) sold SS/DD Passed to Pakistan National Shipping Corp for USD 44 mil, VV Value USD 42.23 mil
MR1 (Chemical/Product) San Sebastian (37,300 DWT, May 2007, Hyundai Mipo) sold SS/DD Passed to Unknown Chinese buyers for USD 13.2 mil, VV Value USD 12.61 mil
Containers: Sub Panamax and Handy values continue to be positive across all ages this week with the larger sizes remaining stable
Sub Panamax X Press Karakoram (2,741 TEU, Jul 2008, Aker Ostsee) sold to Undisclosed buyers for USD 30 mil Inc TC, VV Value USD 27.68 mil
Handy Okee Cuno (1,688 TEU, Dec 2008, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold to Global Feeder Shipping for USD 21 mil, VV Value USD 21.03 mil
