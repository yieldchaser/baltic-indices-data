## Summary
Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 19/08/2025
Image reference: 2025-08-19_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-august-19-2025_img1.png
Bulkers
Values have firmed across the board this week, with older tonnage seeing the largest increase owing to second-hand sales such as Pedhoulas Merchant and Ocean Prince
Kamsarmax BC Pedhoulas Merchant (82,300 DWT, Mar 2006 Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 11.5 mil, VV Value USD 10.99 mil
Supramax BC Ocean Prince (52,500 DWT, Mar 2002, Tadotsu Tsuneishi) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 7.85 mil, VV Value USD 7.47 mil
Tankers
Tanker values remained mostly stable this week as S&P markets stayed quiet with minimal sales amid high newbuilding costs and selective trading
MR2 (Chemical/Product) Nave Equinox (50,900 DWT, Dec 2007, STX Offshore) sold to Unknown Indonesian buyers for USD 14 mil, VV Value USD 15.57 mil
Containers
Midage Handy Container values continue to rise as demand for second-hand vessels stays firm. Post Panamax newbuild values soften following recent orders
Panamax Bach (3,534 TEU, Sept 2009, Shanghai Shipyard) sold to Asian buyers for USD 26 mil inc TC, VV Value USD 29.9 mil
