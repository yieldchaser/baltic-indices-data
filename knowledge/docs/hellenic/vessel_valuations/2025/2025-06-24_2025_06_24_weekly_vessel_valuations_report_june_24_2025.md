## Main
in Weekly Vessel Valuations Report 24/06/2025
Image reference: 2025-06-24_weekly-vessel-valuations-report-june-24-2025_img1.png
Bulkers: Bulker values have remained stable this week, with a decrease in mid age Panamaxes and Supramaxesdue to sales such as Baby Cassiopeia and Star Runner
Capesize BC Partagas (173,900 DWT, May 2004, Shanghai Waigaoqiao Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 14.8 mil, VV Value USD 14.33 mil
Post Panamax BC Baby Cassiopeia (110,900 DWT, Jun 2012, Mitsui Ichihara) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 19 mil, VV Value USD 21.29 mil
Supramax BC Star Runner (57,800 DWT, Sep 2011, Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 12.8 mil, VV Value USD 13.24 mil
Handy BC Amira Sara (28,300 DWT, Jul 2014, Imabari) sold to unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 11.8 mil, VV Value USD 12.35 mil
Tankers: S&P activity has increased in the last week as we have seen deals confirmed from all Tanker sectors with values remaining stable for the most part. Older tonnage VLCCs are achieving lower prices and there is a clear discrepancy between age of vessel and sale price achieved as seen from the recent sales of Papalemos and C Spirit
VLCC Papalemos (319,200 DWT, Aug 2018, Hyundai HI Ulsan Shipyard) sold to DHT Holdings Inc for USD 108 mil, VV Value USD 104.28 mil
VLCC C Spirit (314,000 DWT, Apr 2013, Hyundai HI) sold to Yasa Tankers for USD 67.5 mil, VV Value USD 72.57 mil
Suezmax Nordic Thunder (157,400 DWT, June 2017, Hyundai Samho HI) sold DD Due to Delta for USD 64 mil, VV Value USD 67.07 mil
Aframax Yasa Golden Marmara (110,000 DWT, Jan 2008, Mitsui Ichihara) sold DD Due to Unknown Vietnamese buyers for USD 31 mil, VV Value USD 31.14 mil
LR2 Casper (109,000 DWT, Aug 2010, Hudong Zhonghua) sold SS/DD Due to undisclosed buyers for USD 35 mil, VV Value USD 34.27 mil
MR2 (Chemical/Product) Horizon Aphrodite (50,000 DWT, Sep 2008, SPP) sold to undisclosed buyers for USD 16 mil, VV Value USD 16.61 mil
Containers: Recent sales are nudging down older Handysize and Sub Panamax vessels this week and bolstering older Feedermaxes
Feedermax Contship Key (1,019 TEU, Aug 2004, Kyokuyo) sold for USD 10 mil, VV Value USD 9.3 mil
Handy Containers Galen and Garwood (1,840 TEU, 2007/2008, Hyundai Mipo) sold for USD 18.5 mil each, VV Values USD 19.5 mil and USD 19.6 mil respectively
Handy Containers Escape and Espoir (1,436 TEU, 2011, Jiangsu Guoxin) sold for USD 20 mil each, VV Values USD 20.6 mil, and USD 19.7 mil respectively
Handy Container XH Dolphin (1,728 TEU, Jun 2013, Guangzhou Wenchong) sold for USD 23 mil, VV Value USD 23.6 mil
