## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 05/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures tight by 0.69%. the main contract I2405 closed 879.5. Traders ship according to marketconditions. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 890-900 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 905-910 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (March 2nd to March 8th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.6194 million tons, a decrease of 77100 tons compared to last week's maintenance. The impact of blast furnace maintenance next week (March 9th to March 15th) on molten iron is 1.6164 million tons, a decrease of 3000.
