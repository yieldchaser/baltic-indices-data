## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/08/2024
DCE iron ore futures weakly throughout the day, the main contract I2501 closed 749, down 2.41% for the day. Tradersshowed a general willingness to sell, while steel mills procured at lower prices. The overall market transaction atmosphere was sluggish today. In Shandong, the transaction price of PB fines was 770-775 yuan/mt, down 10-15 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction price of PB fines was 785 yuan/mt, down 10 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to an SMM survey, on August 7, the operating rate of blast furnaces surveyed by SMM was 92.37%, down 0.54% MoM. The capacity utilization rate of blast furnaces was 94.50%, down 0.44% MoM. The average daily pig iron output of the sample steel mills was 2.2672 million mt, down 15,800 mt MoM. However, according to the General Administration of Customs, China's iron ore imports in July increased by 5.4% MoM to 10,281,300 mt. The supply-demand mismatch of iron ore increased, suppressing iron ore prices. In the short term, iron ore prices may continue to fluctuate weakly.
