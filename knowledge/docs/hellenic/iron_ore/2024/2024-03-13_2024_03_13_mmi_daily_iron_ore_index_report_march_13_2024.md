## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling sharply trends，the main contract closed 807.5，an decrease of 2.53% throughout the day;Traders have average enthusiasm for quoting and are willing to ship at an appropriate price; Steel procurement is cautious and not in a hurry, resulting in overall sluggish market transactions. The price of PB powder in Shandong region is between 825-830 yuan/ton, a decrease of 10-15 yuan/ton compared to yesterday; According to SMM research, on March 13th, the blast furnace operating rate recorded by SMM was 89.15%, a decrease of 0.3% compared to the previous month. The utilization rate of blast furnace production capacity was 89.88%, a decrease of 0.76% compared to the previous period.
