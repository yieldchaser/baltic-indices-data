## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures market rose by 3.09% . The main contract I2409 closed at 768; Traders ship according to market conditions；steel mills purchased on demand. Today the overall market transaction atmosphere is acceptable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 779-780 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 790 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron production this week (March 30th to April 5th) was 1.8694 million tons, a decrease of 82.7 thousand tons compared to the previous week. The estimated impact for next week (April 6th to April 12th) is 1.7563 million tons, a further decrease of 113.1 thousand tons compared to this week. Molten iron production is gradually recovering.
