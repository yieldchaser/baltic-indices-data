## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/08/2024
Today, iron ore futures were strong initially but weakened later. The most-traded contract I2501 finally closed at 754.5 yuan/mt, almost flatcompared to yesterday. Traders sold according to market conditions, and steel mills restocked as needed. The overall market transaction atmosphere was sluggish today. In Shandong, PB fines traded at 745-750 yuan/mt, stable compared to yesterday; in Tangshan, PB fines traded at 765 yuan/mt, also stable compared to yesterday. On August 28, the operaƟng rate of blast furnaces at 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 88.23%, down 0.56% MoM. The capacity utilisation rate of blast furnaces was 89.55%, down 1.03% MoM. The daily average pig iron production of the sample steel mills was 2.406 million mt, down 15,000 mt MoM. Based on the current blast furnace maintenance and production resumption plans, pig iron production may bottom out and rebound next week. With the expectation of production resumption, short-term ore prices may remain strong. Future attention should be paid to the end-user demand situation.
