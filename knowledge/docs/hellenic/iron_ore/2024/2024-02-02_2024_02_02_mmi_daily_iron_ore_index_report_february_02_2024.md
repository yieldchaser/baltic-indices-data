## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/02/2024
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, roseing sharply trends in the day. the main contract I2405closed 941. an decrease of 2.23% throughout the day; part of the business quotation shipping mentality turn weak. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 985 yuan/mt; decreased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 995 yuan/mt; decreased 15-20 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of February 2nd, the total inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM was 126.26 million tons, an increase of 1.64 million tons month on month and a decrease of 9.05 million tons year-on-year; The daily average port clearance volume of imported ore is 2.938 million tons, an increase of 5000 tons month on month and 411000 tons year-on-year. As the Spring Festival approaches, some steel mills that have not completed replenishment have accelerated their pace. Overall, the daily average port clearance volume has slightly increased, with a more significant increase in port clearance in North and East China regions; Overall, due to a significant increase in arrivals, port inventory remains in a state of accumulation. Next week, on the eve of the Spring Festival, steel mills will end restocking and market transactions will soon come to a halt. Mineral prices may continue to fluctuate narrowly.
