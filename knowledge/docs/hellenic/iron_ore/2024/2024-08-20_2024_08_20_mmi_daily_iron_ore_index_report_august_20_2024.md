## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 20/08/2024
Iron ore futures ﬂuctuated strongly, with the most-traded contract I2501 closing at 710.5 yuan/mt, up 1.21% for the day. Traders followed the markettrend to sell. Steel mills were cautiously observing, mainly making small and frequent purchases. The overall market transaction atmosphere was relatively average today. In Shandong, PB fines traded at 705- 710 yuan/mt, basically ﬂat compared to yesterday's prices; in Tangshan, PB fines traded at 720-725 yuan/mt, up 0-5 yuan/ slightly, pig iron production remains on a downward trend, and end-user demand has not improved, resulting in insuffi- cient momentum for iron ore prices to rise. Under the pressure of negative feedback, iron ore prices ﬂuctuated at low levels.
