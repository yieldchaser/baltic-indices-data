## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, The main contract I2409 closed at 886.5. an decreaseof 0.06% throughout the day; The enthusiasm of traders for shipping is still acceptable. Some steel mills have a demand for post holiday restocking and purchase as needed. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 882-890 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 905-910 yuan/mt; decreased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (May 4th to May 10th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.2668 million tons, a decrease of 87100 tons compared to last week's maintenance impact.
