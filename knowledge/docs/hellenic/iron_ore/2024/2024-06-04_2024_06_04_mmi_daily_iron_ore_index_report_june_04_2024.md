## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 04/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to fall in a day，the main contract closed 834， an decrease of 2.11% throughout theday; some traders were negative to sell; Some steel mills purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 830-840 yuan/mt; decreased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 855-860 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, this week (June 1st to June 7th), the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron was 1.0117 million tons, a decrease of 63100 tons compared to last week's maintenance. There is still a slight increase in the production of pig iron. However, in recent times, the macro positive sentiment has worsened, and the market's expectations for off-season demand have deviated. The negative feedback logic has advanced, leading to a conƟnuous decline in iron ore prices. In the short term, without any new posiƟve drivers, iron ore prices have continued to operate weakly and fluctuate. Considering the profitability of steel mills, the possibility of negative feedback occurring is extremely low, and the downward space for ore prices is limited.
