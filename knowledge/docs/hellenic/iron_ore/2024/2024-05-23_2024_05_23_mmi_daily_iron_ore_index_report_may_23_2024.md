## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, fall sharply trends in the day by 1.09%. The main contract I2409 closed at 906.5.some traders were negative to sell; Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 885-892 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 910-915 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. Based on the inventory data of the top ten main port iron ore varieties tracked by SMM, the total inventory this week still shows a cumulative trend; The concentrates and fines is more obvious; Lump is relatively stable; However, the cost-effectiveness of domestic pellets is relatively low, and the demand forforeign pellets is increasing, resulting in a decrease in inventory. Pig iron is already at a high level, and inventory is still in a state of accumulation, forming a strong suppression on mineral prices. Adding to the current high price of iron ore, some orders have made profits and left the market, leading to a volatile correction in iron ore prices. But currently, the demand for finished products and inventory data are performing well, and negative feedback from the industrial chain is difficult to form. It is expected that the short-term mining prices will fluctuate at high levels.
