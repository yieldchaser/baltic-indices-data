## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures fall today. The main contract I2409 closed at 899. an decrease of 2.27% throughout the day;some traders were negative to sell; Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 882-887 yuan/mt; decreased 0-10 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900-905 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday. According to SMM shipping data, the total global iron ore shipment was 34.43 million tons, an increase of 10.6% compared to the previous month. Among them, shipments from Australia and Brazil increased significantly month on month, while non mainstream shipments decreased slightly. But current data shows that the proportion of Australia sending to China has significantly decreased; In the future, the actual volume to China remained unchanged month on month. The total amount of iron ore delivered to the port by SMM China was 26.09 million tons, which remained unchanged compared to the previous month. Overall, there is still room for growth in the overall shipment volume of iron ore. Coupled with the short-term peak of molten iron production, there is a risk of slippage this week. It is expected that port inventory will continue to accumulate, suppressing spot prices. However, considering that the profitability of the steel mills is still acceptable, their production enthusiasm is good, and there is also support for ore prices from on-demand procurement. It is expected that there is not much downward space for ore prices in the short term, and they will mainly operate in a volatile manner.
