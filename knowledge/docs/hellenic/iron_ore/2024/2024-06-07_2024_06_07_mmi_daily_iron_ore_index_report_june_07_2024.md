## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower， futures market falling trends，the main contract closed 839.5，an increase of 0.72% throughout the day; Traders ship according to market conditions; The weekly procurement of steel mills has basically ended, with a cautious wait-and-see approach prevailing. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 830-836 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 855-860 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. As of June 7, the total inventory at the 35 ports tracked by SMM was 145.45 million mt, up 900,000 mt from a week ago and up 22.87 million mt YoY. The average daily shipments of imported ore from ports were 3.028 million mt, down 10,000 mt MoM but up 98,000 mt YoY. The high port inventory levels are suppressing the upward potential of iron ore prices. However, the demand for iron ore remains moderate, providing support for the prices.
