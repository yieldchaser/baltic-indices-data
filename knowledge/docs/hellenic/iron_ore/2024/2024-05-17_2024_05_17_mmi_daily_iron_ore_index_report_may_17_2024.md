## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends, The main contract I2409 closed at 891.5. an increase of 2.28% throughout the day .some traders were active to sell; Inquiries from steel mills have decreased, resulting in poor purchasing enthusiasm. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 885-890 yuan/mt; increased 15 -20 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 900-905 yuan/mt; increased 0-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of May 17th, the total inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM was 143.98 million tons, an increase of 1.26 million tons from last week and 19.58 million tons year-on-year; The daily average port clearance volume of imported mines was 2.977 million tons, an increase of 48000 tons month on month and 215000 tons year-on-year.
