## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 27/06/2024

PBF at Shandong port dealt 815-820 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 830-835 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. SMM's total national construction steel inventory contnued to increase by 1.21% month on month. The total inventory of hot coils increased slightly by 9600 tons.
