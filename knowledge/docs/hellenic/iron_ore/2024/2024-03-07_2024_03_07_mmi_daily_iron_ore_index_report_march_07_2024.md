## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, futures in the day by1.83%. the main contract I2405 closed 890.Some traders were active to sell,；Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multifrequency procurement strategy. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 893-898 yuan/mt; increased 0-3 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 760 yuan/mt; increased 13 yuan/mt over yesterday. Based on the inventory data of the top ten main port iron ore varieties tracked by SMM, the port inventory remained in an accumulated state this week. Affected by environmental protection and production restrictions, the demand for lump has increased, and inventory is showing a state of destocking; finesconcentrate, and pellets show.
