## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 24/12/2024
Today, DCE iron ore fluctuated during the day and rose slightly by the close. The most-traded I2505 contract finally settled at783 yuan/mt, up 1.29% throughout the day. Traders sold goods based on market trends, while steel mills remained cautious and purchased as needed. Market transactions were moderate today. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were 775-780 yuan/mt, up 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday. In Tangshan, the transaction prices of PB fines were 785-790 yuan/mt, basically flat compared to yesterday. According to the SMM survey, as of December 18, the operating rate of blast furnaces at 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 85.47%, down 0.76 percentage points MoM. The capacity utilisation rate of blast furnaces was 87.01%, down 0.56 percentage points MoM. The daily pig iron production of the sampled steel mills averaged 2.3476 million mt, down 15,000 mt MoM. Demand for iron ore continued to decline. However, this afternoon, the National Financial Work Conference concluded, and CCTV's interpretation of the meeting highlighted a more proactive fiscal policy, with fiscal funds for 2025 to be allocated earlier, faster, and in larger amounts. This stimulated market sentiment, driving iron ore prices to strengthen.
