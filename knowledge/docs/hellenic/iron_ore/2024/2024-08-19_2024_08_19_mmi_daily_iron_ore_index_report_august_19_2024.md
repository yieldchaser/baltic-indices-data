## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 19/08/2024
Iron ore futures continued to trade in a strong and volatile manner today, with the main contract I2501 closing at 712, a 0.71% increase for the day.Traders followed the trend and shipped their goods accordingly. Steel mills remained cautious and only restocked necessary supplies. Overall, the market had a quiet atmosphere with low trading activity. In Shandong province, the price for PB powder ranged from 705-710 yuan per ton, an increase of 0-5 yuan per ton compared to the price last Friday. In Tangshan, the price for PB powder was at 720 yuan per ton, also a 0-5 yuan per ton increase from last week. According to SMM shipping data, global iron ore shipments totaled 30.54 million tons, a decrease of 4.2% compared to the previous period. Australian shipments saw a slight increase while Brazilian and non-mainstream mine shipments decreased. Chinese iron ore imports reached 25.35 million tons, a 2.89% decrease compared to the previous period. Despite global shipping volumes being low due to seasonality, domestic imports remained high, indicating a surplus in supply. End demand has not improved significantly and steel mills are facing severe losses, leading to a slight decrease in iron production but a narrower decrease compared to last week. Overall, the fundamentals of iron ore have slightly improved compared to last week, providing some support to ore prices.
