## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 09/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends，the main contract closed 815.5,an increase of 5.65% throughout the day.Trade merchants generally have good expectations for the future market, while steel mills have moderate acceptance of current high prices and mainly focus on purchasing based on demand. The overall market transactions are still acceptable. In Shandong, the transaction price of PB powder is in the range of 817-823, which has increased by 10-15 yuan compared to yesterday.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 09/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends，the main contract closed 815.5,an increase of 5.65% throughout the day.Trade merchants generally have good expectations for the future market, while steel mills have moderate acceptance of current high prices and mainly focus on purchasing based on demand. The overall market transactions are still acceptable. In Shandong, the transaction price of PB powder is in the range of 817-823, which has increased by 10-15 yuan compared to yesterday.
