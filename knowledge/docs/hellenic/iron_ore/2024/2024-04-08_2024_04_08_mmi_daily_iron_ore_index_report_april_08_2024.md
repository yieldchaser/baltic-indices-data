## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, The main contract I2409 closed at 791.5; an increase of 3.19% throughout the day;Some traders are optimistic about the market outlook and have decided to hold off on selling, while steel mills have shown limited acceptance of current prices and are mainly purchasing based on demand. Overall, the market trading atmosphere today is still favorable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 807-811 yuan/mt; increased 40 yuan/mt over yesterday.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 08/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, The main contract I2409 closed at 791.5; an increase of 3.19% throughout the day;Some traders are optimistic about the market outlook and have decided to hold off on selling, while steel mills have shown limited acceptance of current prices and are mainly purchasing based on demand. Overall, the market trading atmosphere today is still favorable. PBF at Shandong port dealt 807-811 yuan/mt; increased 40 yuan/mt over yesterday. Global iron ore shipments by sea totaled 33.93 million tons, an increase of 0.3% compared to the previous period. Of this total, shipments from Australia amounted to 18.88 million tons, a decrease of 5.8% from the previous period.
