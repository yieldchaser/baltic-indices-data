## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/07/2024
DCE iron ore futures fall today. the main contract closed 811.5. an decrease of 0.92% throughout the day; sometraders were negative to sell,. Some steel mills purchased on demand. PBF at Shandong port dealt 805-810 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 825-830 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. This week's hot roll and building materials inventory data show some divergence. The total inventory of hot coils continues to increase, but the growth rate has slightly slowed down. However, due to the impact of the new national standard, the speed of destocking building materials inventory has accelerated. In addition, after the decline in iron ore prices, steel mills have increased their procurement of essential goods, which has provided support for mineral prices. However, considering that there were no unexpected favorable policies introduced during the important meeting, expectations fell through; The overlapping industry is in the off-season, and it is expected that iron ore prices may continue to fluctuate at a low level.
