## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures repeatedly, The main contract I2409 closed at 873.5 . some traders were negative to sell；The steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and some steel mills are bidding for procurement. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 865-875 yuan/mt; increased 0-5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 890-905 yuan/mt; increased 0-510 yuan/mt over yesterday. As of May 10th, the total inventory of 35 ports tracked by SMM was 142.71 million tons, a decrease of 750000 tons compared to before the holiday and an increase of 20.29 million tons year-on-year; The daily average port clearance volume of imported mines was 2.929 million tons, an increase of 27000 tons month on month and 66000 tons year-on-year.
