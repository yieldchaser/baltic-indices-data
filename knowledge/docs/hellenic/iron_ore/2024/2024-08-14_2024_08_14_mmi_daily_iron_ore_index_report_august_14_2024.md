## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 14/08/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to decline sharply. The most-traded contract I2501 hit a new low of 706.5 yuan/mt, slightly rebounded in the afternoon,and finally closed at 713 yuan/mt, down 3.32% for the day. Some traders suspended offers; steel mills were cautious and mainly inquired about prices. Overall market transaction sentiment was average today. In Shandong, PB fines traded at 735-740 yuan/mt, down 20- 25 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, PB fines traded at 745-750 yuan/mt, down 20-25 yuan/mt from yesterday. On August 14, the blast furnace operating rate surveyed by SMM was 91.42%, down 0.95% MoM. The daily pig iron production of sample steel mills was 2.2421 million mt, down 25,100 mt MoM. Pig iron production continued to decline significantly. Meanwhile, market rumors about possible crude steel production cuts in Tangshan heightened market concerns, leading to a sharp drop in iron ore prices. Given the potential rebound in pig iron production next week and the approaching peak season, downstream demand may marginally improve, limiting the downside space for iron ore prices, which are expected to remain weak and volatile.
