## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends, the main contract closed 843.5. an decrease of 2.65% throughout the day;some traders were negative to sell, and some traders are unable to sell due to lockdowns; Some steel procurement more cautious, the continuation of a small number of multi-frequency procurement strategy. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 840-850 yuan/mt;decreased 10-20 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 860-870 yuan/mt; decreased 10-20 yuan/mt from last Friday. According to SMM shipping data, the total global iron ore shipment was 30.93 million tons, a decrease of 3.5 million tons compared to the previous month. The shipment volume to Australia has declined, but the shipment to Brazil and non mainstream mines has increased. Although the supply of iron ore has declined this week, the overall supply remains loose, suppressing iron ore prices. In addition, as the impact of the off-season deepens, the market is more concerned about terminal demand, and market sentiment has turned negative. Coupled with no positive macro news, iron ore prices continue to fluctuate weakly.
