## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/12/2024
Today, iron ore ﬂuctuated downward throughout the day, with the most-traded contract I2501 closing at 797.5 yuan/mt, down 0.93% for the day.Traders showed moderate willingness to sell; steel mills remained cautious, with some planning tenders. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 780-785 yuan/mt, down 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, transaction prices for PB fines were 795-800 yuan/mt, down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. Looking ahead to next week, the fundamentals of iron ore are expected to remain stable.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/12/2024
Today, iron ore ﬂuctuated downward throughout the day, with the most-traded contract I2501 closing at 797.5 yuan/mt, down 0.93% for the day.Traders showed moderate willingness to sell; steel mills remained cautious, with some planning tenders. Today's market sentiment was sluggish. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 780-785 yuan/mt, down 0-5 yuan/mt from yesterday; in Tangshan, transaction prices for PB fines were 795-800 yuan/mt, down 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. Looking ahead to next week, the fundamentals of iron ore are expected to remain stable. The main price drivers will be the impact of two important meetings on market sentiment. Next week, market expectations are likely to remain positive, and iron ore prices may ﬂuctuate upward.
