## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/06/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends，the main contract closed 839.5, an increase of 0.96% throughout the day;some traders were active to sell; The inquiry from the steel mill is good, and the willingness to purchase has increased. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 825-835 yuan/mt; increased 2-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 853-860 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. The total inventory of the top ten main port iron ore varieties tracked by SMM continues to accumulate, but the extent of accumulation has narrowed, with a large amount of coarse powder arriving at the port and inventory still accumulating; However, with the import of refined powder, the inventory of pellets and bulk ores has slightly decreased. Overall, the demand for iron ore is still acceptable. And today's steel table data meets market expectations, and the bearish sentiment in the market has improved, leading to a reversal in iron ore prices.
