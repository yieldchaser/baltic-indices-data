## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today, the main contract closed 908, an increase of 1.68% throughout the day; Today's overalltransaction atmosphere is still good, with PB powder prices in Shandong region at 880-895 yuan/ ton, an increase of 5-10 yuan/ton compared to yesterday. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on iron production this week (May 18-24) is 1.0622 million tons, a decrease of 33,200 tons compared to last week. The impact of blast furnace maintenance on iron production next week (May 25-31) is expected to be 1.0554 million tons, a decrease of 6,800 tons compared to this week.
