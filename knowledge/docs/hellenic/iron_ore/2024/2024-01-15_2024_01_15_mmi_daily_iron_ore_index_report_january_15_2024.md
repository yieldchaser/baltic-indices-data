## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/01/2024
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends by 3.17. the main contract I2405 closed 933.Traders ship according to market conditions；Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 997-1018 yuan/mt; decreased 0-21 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1024-1035 yuan/mt; decreased 5-16 yuan/mt from last Friday. SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 30.06 million tons, a slight increase of 3.4% compared to the previous month. The shipment volume from Australia and Brazil has slightly increased, but the proportion of shipments to China has decreased, and it is expected that the arrival volume from China will decline. The arrival volume in this cycle has significantly increased by 13.55% to a high of 2861.88. Loose supply suppresses ore prices, coupled with today's market expectation of MLF not falling as expected, resulitng in a significant downward pressure on ore prices. Short term iron ore prices may still be weakly affected by macroeconomic data and fluctuate.
