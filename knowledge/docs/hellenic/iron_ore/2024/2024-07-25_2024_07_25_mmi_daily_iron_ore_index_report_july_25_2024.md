## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/07/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to fall today %. the main contract closed 764.5. an decrease of 1.55% throughout the day; sometraders were negative to sell, Some steel purchased on demand. PBF at Shandong port dealt 765 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 780-785 yuan/mt; decreased 10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to today's inventory and apparent demand data, the demand for the five major materials has all declined. At present, it is in the off-season, with frequent high temperature and rainstorm warnings, the market estimates that the terminal demand may further weaken in the future. In addition, the alternation of new and old national standards has a significant impact on the rebar market, with strong selling sentiment and widespread pessimism, leading to a sustained decline in iron ore prices.
