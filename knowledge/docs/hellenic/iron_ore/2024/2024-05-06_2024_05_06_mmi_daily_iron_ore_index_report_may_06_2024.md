## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 06/05/2024
DCE iron ore futures market roseing sharply trends by 2.63%. some traders ship according to market conditions;Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 885-895 yuan/mt; increased 10-20 yuan/mt before the holiday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 910-920 yuan/mt; increased 10-20 yuan/mt before the holiday. According to SMM shipping data, the global shipment volume last week was 33.74 million tons, an increase of 6.4% compared to the previous week. The shipment volume from Australia and Brazil has slightly increased, but the proportion to China has slightly declined.
