## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures continued to fall today. The main contract I2409 closed at 849. an decreaseof 1.91% throughout the day; some traders were negative to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 855-865 yuan/mt; decreased 10-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 895-900 yuan/mt; decreased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM statistics, from April 20th to April 26th, the impact of pig iron output caused by blast furnace maintenance was 148.39 million mt, a decrease of 19.32 million mt from a week ago.
