## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/03/2024
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract I2405 closed 798.5. an increase of 0.38%throughout the day; Traders ship according to market conditions; Inquiries from steel mills have decreased, resulting in poor purchasing enthusiasm. PBF at Shandong port dealt 780-805 yuan/mt; increased 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 795-810 yuan/mt; increased 0-10 yuan/mt from last Friday. SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 29.65 million tons, a decrease of 2.6% compared to the previous month. Among them, 18.37 million tons were shipped from Australia, a decrease of 0.9% month on month; Australia shipped 15.11 million tons to China, accounting for 82.2% of the total shipment to Australia, an increase of 4.1% month on month.
