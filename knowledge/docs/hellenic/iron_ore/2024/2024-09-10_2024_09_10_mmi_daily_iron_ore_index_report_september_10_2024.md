## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/09/2024
Today, iron ore ﬂuctuated upward and plunged at the close. The most-traded contract I2501 finally settled at 675 yuan/mt, dropping 0.07%throughout the day. Traders were fairly active in oﬄoading their stocks. After the steel mills lowered prices, their cautious sentiment grew stronger, leading to a reduction in purchase willingness. Today's market trading atmosphere was average. The transaction price of PB fines in Shandong was around 678-690 yuan/mt, down 0-2 yuan/mt compared to yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction price of PB fines was around 692-705 yuan/mt, down 0-8 yuan/mt compared to yesterday. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on pig iron production this week was 1.4196 million mt, down 35,800 mt WoW. Due to the slow growth in downstream demand, the resumption of blast furnace operations at steel mills has been delayed, making a significant increase in pig iron production unlikely in the short term. The upward momentum for ore prices is lacking, and they may continue to ﬂuctuate at low levels in the near term.
