## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 11/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today, the main contract closed 826, an increase of 1.29% throughout the day。Traders are generally active inshipping, while steel mills are more hesitant and still mainly purchasing on demand. The overall market trading atmosphere is average. In Shandong province, the transaction prices for powder metallurgy iron (PB powder) are 825-830 yuan per ton and for ultrafine iron powder are 660-665 yuan, with prices rising slightly by 1-5 yuan compared to yesterday.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 11/04/2024
DCE iron ore futures rose largely today, the main contract closed 826, an increase of 1.29% throughout the day。Traders are generally active inshipping, while steel mills are more hesitant and still mainly purchasing on demand. The overall market trading atmosphere is average. In Shandong province, the transaction prices for powder metallurgy iron (PB powder) are 825-830 yuan per ton and for ultrafine iron powder are 660-665 yuan, with prices rising slightly by 1-5 yuan compared to yesterday. From today's apparent steel data, downstream demand has shown some improvement, combined with the increase in blast furnace iron production at current steel mills, which may lead to increased demand for iron powder and provide some support for prices.
