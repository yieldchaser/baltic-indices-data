## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 17/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures tight by 2.12%. the main contract I2401 closed 862, some traders were active to sell,steel mills purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 945 yuan/mt; increased 10yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Shandong port dealt 959- 960 yuan/mt; increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. According to SMM's tracking of blast furnace maintenance data, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron this week was 1.192 million tons, an increase of 105700 tons compared to last week's maintenance impact. In terms of the impact of maintenance, the reduction in molten iron production during this maintenance is still relatively small. Considering the current low inventory in ports, the pressure on ore prices is not significant. However, considering that Shanxi has issued another environmental production restriction policy today and that the air quality in Tangshan, Hebei has been average recently, it cannot be ruled out that the intensity of environmental production restriction has increased. In addition, with the increase in ore prices and the deepening of losses in steel mills, there is still room for a decrease in hot metal production due to the inspection of blast furnaces or the postponement of resumption of production and the introduction of new blast furnace maintenance plans. In the short term, the difficulty of raising ore prices may increase, or the operation may continue to fluctuate.
