## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 26/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures open lower and go higher, the main contract I2401 closed 876.5,an increase of 0.69% throughout the day. Traders ship according to market conditions. Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 934 yuan/mt; increased 5 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 950 yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. According to the inventory data of SMM's top ten major ports by category, the inventory of all four major varieties has slightly decreased in this cycle, with the largest reduction in lump ore inventory. This is also due to the recent tightening of environmental production restrictions in Shanxi and Tangshan. The low inventory of various types of ports still supports mining prices. Stacked with higher than expected terminal demand, it is expected that mining prices will continue to fluctuate strongly tomorrow.
