## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.74 % today, the main contract closed at 819.The steel mills are active to purchase. The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 909-910 yuan/mt. On the surface of the news, there has been another thunderstorm news from the real estate sector, causing market discussions and generating a certain level of pessimism. Based on the maintenance data of steel mills' blast furnaces tracked by SMM, the impact of blast furnace maintenance this week was 923700 tons, an increase of 57600 tons compared to last week's maintenance. It is expected that the daily average iron production of blast furnaces will slightly decrease this week. Last week, the inventory of Port 35 still slightly decreased, but some ports in South China have been affected by typhoons recently, which may affect the arrival of iron ore. It is expected that there will be no significant accumulation of iron ore in the port this week. It is expected that the short-term iron ore prices will continue to fluctuate widely.
