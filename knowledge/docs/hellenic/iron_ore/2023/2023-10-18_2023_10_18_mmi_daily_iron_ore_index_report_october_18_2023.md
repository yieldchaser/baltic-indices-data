## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 18/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures market falling trends by 0.4%. the main contract I2401 closed 861, some traders were negative to sell,Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 929-940 yuan/mt; decreased 5-15 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Shandong port dealt 950- 960 yuan/mt; decreased 0-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. The real estate data released today remains weak, with poor demand for finished products and a weakening of market sentiment. But fundamentally speaking, the shipping volume of mainstream mines in the third quarter is relatively stable, and although there has been an increase in blast furnace maintenance, the production of molten iron is still at a high level. With low port inventory, ore prices have been supported, with a lower decline than finished products. Considering the deepening losses caused by raw material compression in steel mills, the increasing willingness to maintain blast furnaces, and the weak willingness to purchase iron ore in stock, the ore prices are operating under pressure and fluctuating.
