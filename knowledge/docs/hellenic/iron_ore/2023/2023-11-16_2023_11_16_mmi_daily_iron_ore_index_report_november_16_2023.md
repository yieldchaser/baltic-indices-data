## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 16/11/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 1.53 % today, the main contract closed at 965. The steel mills are not active topurchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is general. PBF at Shandong port deal 985 yuan/mt, PBF at Tangshan port deal 995- 1000 yuan/mt. Yesterday's meeting of the National Development and Reform Commission mentioned iron ore, which cooled market sentiment and led to a rebound in the futures market. According to the inventory data of the top ten ports tracked by SMM, the total inventory increased by 0.4% month on month to 76.79 million tons this week, with only crude powder inventory decreasing. Although the profits of the steel mill have been restored, the strong prices of raw materials have dragged down the steel mill's willingness to purchase. In the short term, it is expected that mining prices will weaken and return to a reasonable range.
