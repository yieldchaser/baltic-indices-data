## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 20/12/2023
DCE iron ore have stopped falling and rebounded, the main contract I2405 closed 939, an increaseof 1.46% throughout the day. some traders were active to sell, Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchased on demand. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 991-995 yuan/mt; increased 6-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1005-1008 yuan/mt; increased 5-8 yuan/mt over yesterday. The operating rate of blast furnaces according to SMM statistics is 89.74%, a decrease of 0.3% compared to last week. The utilization rate of blast furnace production capacity is 90.42%, an increase of 0.08% compared to the previous period. The daily average iron production of the sample steel plant was 2.1689 million tons, an increase of 0.0600 tons compared to the previous period. However, the overseas shipment of iron ore is still in the year-end surge stage, with supply tending to be loose, demand continuing to weaken, and support for iron ore being weak. But approaching the pre holiday restocking, market expectations are high, and ore prices are prone to rise but difficult to fall.
