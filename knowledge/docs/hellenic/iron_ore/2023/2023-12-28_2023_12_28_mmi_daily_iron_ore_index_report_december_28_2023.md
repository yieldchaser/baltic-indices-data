## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 28/12/2023
DCE iron ore futures open higher and go lower, futures fall in the day. the main contract I2405 closed 966.some traders quotation is still stronger. total transactions remained insipid. Steel mills are cautious and cautious today, with a focus on purchasing at lower prices. today's overall market transaction atmosphere in general. PBF at Shandong port dealt 1018-1025 yuan/mt; decreased 2-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. SSF at Shandong port dealt 906 yuan/mt; decreased 2-7 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1047-1050 yuan/mt; decreased 0-3 yuan/mt over yesterday. This week, the building materials table needs to continue to decline, and the overall social inventory of steel has turned from decline to increase. The fundamentals have deteriorated, and the seasonal off-season characteristics are obvious. As the off -season market deepens, the speed of steel inventory accumulation is gradually accelerating, suppressing market prices and causing weak fluctuations in mining prices. In the later stage, iron ore pre holiday restocking will gradually begin, and spot prices still have support, continuing to fluctuate.
