## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 25/10/2023
DCE iron ore futures market rose by 3.32%. the main contract I2401 closed 872, some traders were active to sell,Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. Less inquiries throughout the day. total transactions is poor. PBF at Shandong port dealt 929-930yuan/mt; the same as yesterday. Yesterday, the sixth session of the 14th National People's Congress closed, and the resolution on approving the issuance of additional treasury bond by the State Council and the central budget adjustment plan for 2023 was passed; At the same time, there is news in the market that the central government will issue an additional 1 trillion yuan of 2023 treasury bond in the fourth quarter of this year, and it is planned to use 500 billion yuan this year. Macro policies are favorable, boosting market sentiment. Combined with SMM tracking statistics this week, the operating rate of steel mills' blast furnaces remains flat, and daily iron ore production only drops by 2300 tons. The overall demand for iron ore is still relatively strong, which provides support for prices. Considering that steel mills' losses deepen and suppress ore prices, there is limited room for iron ore to rise. It is expected that short-term mining prices will continue to fluctuate.
