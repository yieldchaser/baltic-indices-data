## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/12/2023
DCE iron ore futures market rose by 3.53%. the main contract I2405 closed 968, Some traders adopt separate quotations；Some steel mills tended to be wait-and-see, and purchasing enthusiasm is not high. total transactions remained insipid. PBF at Shandong port dealt 996-1000 yuan/mt; increased 5-10 yuan/mt over yesterday. PBF at Tangshan port dealt 1015-1028 yuan/mt; increased 10-18 yuan/mt over yesterday. The inventory of SMM's ten port sub varieties is still in a slight destocking state. The destocking in this cycle is mainly due to a significant decline in arrivals last week due to port congestion. From the perspective of variety structure, many regions in Hebei have once again experienced environmental production restrictions. Although the cost-effectiveness of lump ore and pellets is not high, there has been a slight increase in demand.
