## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/11/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.48 % today, the main contract closed at 923.5. The steelmills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is less. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron this week was 1.4927 million tons, an increase of 126200 tons compared to last week's maintenance; The impact of blast furnace maintenance next week on molten iron is 1.3226 million tons, which will decrease compared to this week.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 07/11/2023
DCE iron ore futures declined by 0.48 % today, the main contract closed at 923.5. The steelmills are not active to purchase.The overall trading sentiment of the market is less. According to SMM statistics, the impact of blast furnace maintenance on molten iron this week was 1.4927 million tons, an increase of 126200 tons compared to last week's maintenance; The impact of blast furnace maintenance next week on molten iron is 1.3226 million tons, which will decrease compared to this week. As the year-end surge of overseas mines approaches, it is expected that port inventory will accumulate again. The news gradually digested, and short-term iron ore prices fluctuated.
