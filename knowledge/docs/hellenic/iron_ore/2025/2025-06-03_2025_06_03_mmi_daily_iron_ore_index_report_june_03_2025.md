## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 03/06/2025
Today, DCE iron ore futures opened lower and closed lower, with the most-traded contract I2509 eventually closing at 695.5, down 1.14% for the day.Traders sold goods according to market conditions; steel mills adopted a wait-and-see aƫtude cautiously, with general purchase willingness. The market transaction atmosphere was sluggish. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were in the range of 726-730 yuan/mt, dropping slightly by 5-10 yuan/mt compared to the prices before the holiday. In the Tangshan region, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 740-745 yuan/mt, also dropping slightly by 5-10 yuan/mt compared to the prices before the holiday. Last week, SMM's total global iron ore shipments reached 34.98 million mt, down 420,000 mt WoW. SMM's total iron ore arrivals in China reached 25.41 million mt, with a slight decrease of 60,000 mt WoW. Currently, the supply side has not yet reached its peak level, and there is still room for growth in the later period. Due to the impact of tariff policy on market sentiment during the holiday, combined with the "strong supply and weak demand" paƩern in the iron ore fundamentals, it is expected that ore prices will remain in the doldrums in the short term.
