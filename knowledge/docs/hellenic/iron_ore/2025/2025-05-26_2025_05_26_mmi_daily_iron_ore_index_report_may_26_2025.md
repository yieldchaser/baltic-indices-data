## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 26/05/2025
Today, DCE iron ore futures continued to decline, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 706, down 2.21% for the day. Traders' willingnessto sell was average; steel mills adopted a wait-and-see aƫtude, with relatively low procurement enthusiasm. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were in the range of 740-745 yuan/mt, down 10-15 yuan/mt from Friday last week. In Tangshan, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 755-760 yuan/mt, also down 10-15 yuan/mt from Friday last week. Last week, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 35.4 million mt, increasing by 1.35 million mt WoW, with the growth rate slightly narrowing. Among them, Brazil saw the largest increase in shipments. SMM's total iron ore arrivals in China were 25.48 million mt, decreasing by 800,000 mt WoW. The supply of iron ore continued to increase slightly.
