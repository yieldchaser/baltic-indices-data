Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/09/2025

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/09/2025
Iron ore futures showed slight strength today, with the most-traded contract I2601 closing at 771.5, up 0.06% from the previous trading day.Traders sold at market prices, while steel mills purchased as needed, with inquiries declining from yesterday. Market trading sentiment remained moderate. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines stood at 765-770 yuan/mt, up 0-3 yuan/mt from yesterday. In Tangshan, PB fines traded around 780-785 yuan/mt, up 5-10 yuan/mt from yesterday. The resurgence of conflict in the Middle East today drove crude oil prices significantly higher, which in turn lent some strength to ore prices. However, SMM data on blast furnace maintenance impact showed this week's affected volume reached 1.5152 million mt, up 361,900 mt WoW. This continues to exert short-term pressure on ore prices, with a meaningful rebound only expected after environmental protection-driven production restrictions are lifted.
