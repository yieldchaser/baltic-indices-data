## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/07/2025

Traders were actively offloading positions, while speculative trading remained weak. Some steel mills purchased as needed, but most adopted a cautious wait-and-see approach, with weak purchase willingness. The overall market sentiment was subdued.
