## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/04/2025
Today, the DCE continued to fluctuate upward. The most-traded contract I2505 closed at 791.5,with a daily increase of 1.09%. Traders sold goods according to market conditions, while steel mills restocked as needed, with moderate purchase willingness. The market trading atmosphere was generally flat. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 785-790 yuan/mt, stable compared to yesterday. In Tangshan, the transaction prices of PB fines were around 800 yuan/mt, also stable compared to yesterday. According to the SMM survey, on April 2, the blast furnace operating rate of 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 87.49%, up 0.02 percentage points MoM. The blast furnace capacity utilization rate was 89.40%, up 0.17 percentage points MoM. The daily average pig iron production of the sample steel mills was 2.4131 million mt, up 4,600 mt MoM. During this period, 5 blast furnaces underwent maintenance, while 4 blast furnaces resumed production.
