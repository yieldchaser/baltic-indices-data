## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 02/01/2025
Today, Dalian iron ore futures opened lower but closed higher, fluctuating upward throughout the day. The most-traded contract I2505 finallysettled at 782 yuan/mt, rising slightly by 0.64% for the day. Traders showed moderate willingness to sell, while some steel mills purchased as needed. The market trading atmosphere was moderate today. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were around 780 yuan/mt, up by 5 yuan/mt compared to Tuesday; in Tangshan, the transaction prices of PB fines were 790-795 yuan/mt, also up by 5 yuan/mt compared to Tuesday. Industry data released today showed a turning point in total inventory, with a slight accumulation beginning. Apparent demand declined significantly, and the off-season effect became more evident. However, pig iron production is expected to stabilize next week, and raw material restocking by steel mills is anticipated to increase. Iron ore prices have some support and may continue a fluctuating upward trend in the short term.
