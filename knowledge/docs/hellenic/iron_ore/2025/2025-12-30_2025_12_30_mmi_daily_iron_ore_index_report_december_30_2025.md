## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 30/12/2025
Iron ore prices retreated from their high levels today, showing overall weak performance throughout the session.The main contract I2605 closed at 789, down 0.44% from the previous trading day. Traders followed market trends in their sales, while steel mills procured at lower prices, with inquiries increasing. Market trading activity remained rela vely active. In the Shandong region, the transaction price for PB fines ranged from 793 to 800 yuan, down 3-7 yuan/ton from yesterday. In the Hebei region, the transaction price for PB fines ranged from 810 to 820 yuan/ton, up 2-5 yuan/ton from yesterday. According to SMM data on blast furnace maintenance impacts, this week's affected production volume was 1.856 million tons, a decrease of 79,000 tons from the previous week. Despite ongoing environmental production restrictions in the Hebei region, the current restrictions have been slightly relaxed compared to the previous round, primarily involving a 30% reduction in sintering machine operations, with no impact on blast furnaces. Entering January, some of the blast furnaces under maintenance are scheduled to resume operations. Additionally, expectations for pre-holiday inventory restocking have strengthened. Coupled with opmistimacroeconomic news, market senment has been boosted, driving iron ore prices to fluctuate upward. It is expected that post-holiday ore prices will still have upward momentum.
