## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/02/2025
Today, iron ore futures continued to fluctuate upward, with the most-traded I2505 contract closing at 838.5 yuan/mt,up 1.51% for the day. Traders actively sold goods, while some steel mills locked in profits, showing moderate purchase willingness. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 830-835 yuan/mt, up 1-5 yuan/mt compared to yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction prices for PB fines were around 840-845 yuan/mt, basically flat compared to yesterday.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/02/2025
Today, iron ore futures continued to fluctuate upward, with the most-traded I2505 contract closing at 838.5 yuan/mt,up 1.51% for the day. Traders actively sold goods, while some steel mills locked in profits, showing moderate purchase willingness. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were 830-835 yuan/mt, up 1-5 yuan/mt compared to yesterday; in Tangshan, the transaction prices for PB fines were around 840-845 yuan/mt, basically flat compared to yesterday. As of February 21, SMM data showed that total inventory at 35 ports had decreased to 149.18 million mt, down 370,000 mt WoW. Next week, due to the impact of previous shipments, port arrivals may slightly decline, and port inventory still has the potential for destocking. Coupled with the upcoming Two Sessions, the market remains optimistic about favorable policies. However, considering that current iron ore prices are at a high level, there is significant resistance to further upward movement. Iron ore prices are expected to fluctuate at high levels next week.
