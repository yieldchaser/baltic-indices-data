## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 22/01/2025
Today, iron ore fluctuated upward. The most-traded contract I2505 finally closed at 800.5 yuan/mt, down 0.44% throughoutthe day. Traders showed moderate willingness to sell. Steel mills remained cautious and inquiries were limited. The market transaction atmosphere was moderate today. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction prices of PB fines were 788-792 yuan/mt, down 5 yuan/mt from yesterday. According to an SMM survey, as of January 22, the operating rate of blast furnaces at 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 85.60%, up 0.46 percentage points MoM. The capacity utilisation rate of blast furnaces was 87.85%, up 0.87 percentage points MoM. The daily pig iron production of sample steel mills was 2.3641 million mt, up 15,300 mt MoM. Currently, pig iron production at steel mills continues to rise, providing some support for the demand for iron ore concentrates. It is expected that iron ore prices may fluctuate upward in the short term.
