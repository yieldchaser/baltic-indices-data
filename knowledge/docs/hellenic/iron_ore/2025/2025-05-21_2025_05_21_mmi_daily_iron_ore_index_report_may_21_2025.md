## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/05/2025
Today, the Dalian iron ore futures continued to hold up well, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 728.5, up 0.76% for the day.Traders sold their goods in line with market conditions, while steel mills adopted a wait-and-see attitude, with their purchase willingness weakening somewhat. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction price of PB fines was around 765 yuan/mt, basically flat compared to yesterday's price.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 21/05/2025
Today, the Dalian iron ore futures continued to hold up well, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 728.5, up 0.76% for the day.Traders sold their goods in line with market conditions, while steel mills adopted a wait-and-see attitude, with their purchase willingness weakening somewhat. The market transaction atmosphere was average. In the Shandong region, the mainstream transaction price of PB fines was around 765 yuan/mt, basically flat compared to yesterday's price. In the Tangshan region, the transaction price of PB fines was around 775 yuan/mt, also basically flat compared to yesterday's price. Today, the market learned that Guinea had designated several mining rights as strategic reserve areas, including iron ore projects. Coupled with the prolonged maintenance period due to the collapse of port equipment in Peru earlier, the market was concerned about a reduction in iron ore supply, supporting a slight rise in ore prices.
