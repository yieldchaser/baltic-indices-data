## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 23/04/2025
Today, the DCE iron ore futures surged significantly, with the most-traded contract I2509 closing at 727.5, up 2.11% for the day.Traders were active in selling, while steel mills mainly purchased as needed. The market transaction atmosphere was moderate. In Shandong, the mainstream transaction price for PB fines was around 775 yuan/mt, up 3-5 yuan/mt from yesterday. In Tangshan, the transaction price for PB fines was around 775-780 yuan/mt, down 2-5 yuan/mt from yesterday. US President Trump spoke publicly on Tuesday, April 22, acknowledging that the current tariffs on Chinese exports to the US are too high and are expected to be significantly reduced. This marks a sottening of Trump's stance on his signature tariff policy. Market sentiment was optimistic, driving a significant rise in the futures market. Additionally, according to an SMM survey, on April 23, the blast furnace operating rate of 242 steel mills surveyed by SMM was 88.58%, up 0.8 percentage points MoM. The blast furnace capacity utilization rate was 90.49%, up 0.66 percentage points MoM. The daily average pig iron production of the sampled steel mills was 2.4415 million mt, up 16,700 mt MoM.
