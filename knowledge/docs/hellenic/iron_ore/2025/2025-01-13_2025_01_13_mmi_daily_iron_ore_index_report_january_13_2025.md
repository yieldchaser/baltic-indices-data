## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 13/01/2025
Today, iron ore fluctuated upward. The most-traded contract I2505 closed at 768.5 yuan/mt,up 1.92% for the day. Traders showed moderate enthusiasm for selling, while steel mills remained cautious and purchased as needed. The market transaction atmosphere was moderate today. In Shandong, mainstream transaction prices for PB fines were at 770-775 yuan/mt, up 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday; in Tangshan, transaction prices for PB fines were around 790 yuan/mt, also up 10-15 yuan/mt from last Friday. In the latest cycle, SMM's global iron ore shipments totaled 28.78 million mt, down 10.3% WoW. Among them, shipments from Australia were 18.11 million mt, up slightly by 0.7% WoW; shipments from Brazil were 5.71 million mt, down significantly by 25.1% WoW. SMM's total iron ore port arrivals in China were 26.89 million mt, up slightly by 1.63% WoW. Although shipments declined significantly this cycle, considering the high shipment volumes throughout December, January port arrivals are expected to remain at high levels, continuing to exert pressure on ore prices. Given the market's potential anticipation of the end of restocking, caution is advised regarding the risk of ore prices pulling back after a rebound.
