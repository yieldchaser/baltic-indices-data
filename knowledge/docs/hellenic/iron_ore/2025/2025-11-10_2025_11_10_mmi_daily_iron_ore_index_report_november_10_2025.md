## Summary
Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/11/2025
The most-traded iron ore futures contract on the DCE weakened in the morning session and rebounded slightly in the afternoon.The most-traded I2601 contract closed at 765 yuan/mt, down 0.07% from the previous working day. Mainstream spot cargoes at ports rose 0-5 yuan/mt from the previous working day, with transac on prices of PB fines in Shandong at 766-775 yuan/mt, up 2-5 yuan/mt WoW from last Friday; transac on prices of PB fines in Hebei at 779-785 yuan/mt, up 0-5 yuan/mt WoW from last Friday. Last week, global iron ore shipments totaled 33.49 million mt, down about 3.9% WoW, while China's iron ore arrivals totaled 30.36 million mt, up 1.6% WoW.

## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 10/11/2025
The most-traded iron ore futures contract on the DCE weakened in the morning session and rebounded slightly in the afternoon.The most-traded I2601 contract closed at 765 yuan/mt, down 0.07% from the previous working day. Mainstream spot cargoes at ports rose 0-5 yuan/mt from the previous working day, with transac on prices of PB fines in Shandong at 766-775 yuan/mt, up 2-5 yuan/mt WoW from last Friday; transac on prices of PB fines in Hebei at 779-785 yuan/mt, up 0-5 yuan/mt WoW from last Friday. Last week, global iron ore shipments totaled 33.49 million mt, down about 3.9% WoW, while China's iron ore arrivals totaled 30.36 million mt, up 1.6% WoW. Iron ore supply con nued to increase; however, demand remained weak. According to the SMM survey, blast furnace mainte nance was mostly concentrated in late November and December, and hot metal produc on was expected to maintain high levels with narrow fluctua ons this week. Spot iron ore prices found support, and downside room for iron ore prices may be limited in the short term, likely fluctua ng rangebound in the doldrums.
