## Main
in Chinese iron ore and steelmaking prices 15/12/2025
Today, the DCE iron ore contract fluctuated at weak bias as the main contract I2605 finally closed at 753, down 0.92% fromthe previous working day. Traders shipped goods as demand; steel mills purchased according to demand, and the inquiry volume was better. Overall, the market transaction atmosphere is general. The deal price of PB fines in Shandong area was 770-775 yuan, down 5-10 yuan/ton from last Friday; The transaction price of PB fines in Hebei area was 785-790 yuan/ ton, down 5-10 yuan/ton from last Friday; According to SMM shipping data last week, the total global iron ore shipments were 35.87 million tons, an increase of 2.57 million tons from the previous week, an increase of 7.7%. SMM China's total iron ore port arrivals were 26.88 million tons, down 340,000 tones from the previous week, a slight decrease of 1.2%. The supply has decreased slightly, but the impact is limited. However, a new round of environmental protection production restrictions in the north has arrived. At present, most of the production restrictions are mainly on sintering machines, and some steel mills in some areas are cooperating with environmental protection production restrictions for major repairs. Or shut down the furnace as required. Affected by this, the demand for iron ore will still decline in the short term.
